## Game 1: Donut Dare Roulette

**How to Play:**
1. Arrange a dozen donuts on a tray. Under each donut, place a folded dare or drinking challenge (think: “Take two sips,” “Swap a donut with someone,” “Share your wildest meme,” or an NSFW dare).
2. Players take turns picking a donut and reveal the challenge underneath.
3. Complete the dare or drink the number of sips written on the card.
4. The player who gets the most wild challenge wins a bonus donut—or another round!

## Game 2: Donut Stack Sips

**How to Play:**
1. Give each player a donut and a drink.
2. The challenge? Stack your donut on your forehead and balance it for 10 seconds. 
3. If it falls, take a drink. If you succeed, assign two drinks to someone else!
4. Keep going with different balancing acts—on your nose, chin, or even elbow.

## Game 3: Donut Hole Pong

**How to Play:**
1. Set up a pong table, but swap the traditional cups for small plates with donut holes.
2. Players toss ping pong balls, aiming for the donut holes.
3. Make a shot? The other team eats a donut hole and takes a drink.
4. Last team standing (with the least donut holes left) wins.
