## Party Drinking Games for Your Black Friday Streaming Marathon

### 1. **Binge Battle Royale**

**Inspired by:** Fortnite drinking game, viral drinking games

**How to Play:**

- Pick a competitive reality show, eSports stream, or any action-packed series.
- Everyone chooses a “champion” (contestant, character, or player).
- Drink whenever your champion:
  - Makes a big move (wins a round, scores, gets dramatic)
  - Drops a meme-worthy quote
  - Gets eliminated — finish your drink!

**Materials:** Drinks, streaming device, show of choice

### 2. **Meme Scene Sips**

**Inspired by:** Meme drinking games, dare challenges

**How to Play:**

- Before the show, everyone predicts which scenes will go viral or become memes.
- Each time a predicted meme moment happens, the person who called it gives out sips to the group.
- No meme-worthy moment? The predictor must take a dare from the custom pack or try an NSFW dare if you’re feeling bold.

**Materials:** Drinks, streaming device, list of meme predictions, [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com)

### 3. **Streaming Dare Roulette**

**Inspired by:** Dare challenges, custom drinking game builder

**How to Play:**

- At random intervals (or after each episode), spin the digital dare wheel on Drinking Dojo.
- Complete the dare within the next scene — anything from singing the theme song to recreating a dramatic monologue.
- Refuse a dare? Take two drinks!

**Materials:** Drinks, streaming device, [Drinking Dojo dare wheel](https://drinkingdojo.com)

### 4. **Couples Couch Challenge**

**Inspired by:** Couples drinking games, house party ideas

**How to Play:**

- Ideal for streaming date nights or double dates.
- Each couple picks a word or phrase that pops up often in the show.
- Whenever it’s said, the couple shares a drink and must complete a mini-challenge (like swapping seats or answering a spicy question).

**Materials:** Drinks, streaming device, list of trigger words
