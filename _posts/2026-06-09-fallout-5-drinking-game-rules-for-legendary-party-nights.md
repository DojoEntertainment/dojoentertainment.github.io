## How to Play the Fallout 5 Party Drinking Game

This party drinking game is all about mixing classic Fallout mayhem with drinking challenges and meme-worthy moments. Here’s how to set it up:

### Basic Rules for Fallout 5 Drinking Games

Assign everyone a controller or take turns exploring the wasteland. Every time something on the list happens, take a drink or complete a dare. Ready? Let’s go!

#### Take a Sip When:

- You pick up a completely useless item (who really needs another rusty spoon?)
- Dogmeat does literally anything adorable
- Someone accidentally triggers a trap
- You get caught stealing
- Your character’s dialogue is accidentally savage

#### Take Two Sips When:

- You level up (cheers to progress)
- You pick the *wrong* lock and break your bobbypin
- A radstorm hits
- You find a hidden meme reference or Easter egg

#### Finish Your Drink or Do a Dare When:

- You die in the most ridiculous way possible
- You get caught in an awkward NPC conversation loop
- You accidentally nuke yourself (it happens to the best of us)
- Someone in the group completes a wild NSFW dare challenge
