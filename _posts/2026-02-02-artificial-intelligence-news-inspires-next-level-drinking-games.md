## Introducing: The AI News Drinking Challenge

Ready to take your party drinking games to the next level? Here’s how you can turn hot AI headlines into a hilarious, meme-packed house party idea.

### What You’ll Need

- Your favorite drinks (beer, cocktails, juice—we don’t judge)
- A phone or laptop for browsing AI news (bonus points for meme generators)
- Dare cards (DIY or use [Drinking Dojo’s custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com))
- Friends ready for anything

### How to Play

**Step 1: Gather the Crew**  
Form a circle (virtual or IRL) and make sure everyone’s got a drink.

**Step 2: Scroll for the Latest AI News**  
Open your favorite news site or TikTok feed and search for trending artificial intelligence news. Each round, one person reads out a headline.

**Step 3: React and Drink**  
After hearing the headline, players must react according to these rules:

- **If it sounds fake but is real:** Everyone takes a sip.
- **If it’s about AI creating memes:** Last person to show a meme on their phone does a dare.
- **If the headline mentions robots taking over:** Person with the best robot dance gives out two drinks.
- **If it’s about AI and jobs:** Everyone with a LinkedIn profile drinks.
- **Bonus:** If someone finds a *Fortnite drinking game* or anything about AI in gaming, the finder gets to make a custom dare for the group.

**Step 4: The Dare Challenge**  
Pull a dare card for extra spice. Think NSFW dares (if you’re feeling brave), meme drinking games, or couples drinking games for those in a flirty mood.

**Step 5: Repeat and Get Wild**  
Keep the rounds going until you’re ready to crown the Meme Master of the night.
