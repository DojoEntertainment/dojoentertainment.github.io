### Step 1: Set Up Your Squad

Get your group together—this game works for 2 to 10+ players. Perfect for dorm rooms, house parties, or even wedding party games (because who says grandma can’t cheer for Djokovic?).

### Step 3: The Rules

Every time one of these ATP Rome moments happens, drink up:

- **Ace Serve:** Everyone takes a sip.  
- **Double Fault:** Last person to shout “Rally!” does a dare.  
- **Player Challenges a Call:** Group votes—if overruled, the challenger drinks twice.  
- **Long Rally (10+ shots):** Everyone drinks until the rally ends.  
- **Commentator Says “Clay Court Specialist”:** First person to point at the screen gives out a drink.  
- **Rain Delay:** Social drink!  
- **Wild Fan in the Crowd:** Dare challenge for the person who spots them first.  

Add your own meme drinking games—like “Drink every time someone mentions Nadal’s forehand” or “Sip for every close-up of angry coaches.”

### Step 4: Dare Challenges

Spice it up with ATP Rome-specific dares. Try these:

- **Imitate a tennis grunt until your next turn**  
- **Speak in a faux Italian accent for one round**  
- **Serve a drink to another player—if you miss pouring, you drink**  
- **NSFW dare? Pull one from the Drinking Dojo pack if you dare**
