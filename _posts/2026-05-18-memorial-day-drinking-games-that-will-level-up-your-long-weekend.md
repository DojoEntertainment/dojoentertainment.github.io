### 1. Stars and Shots

**Perfect for:** Backyard BBQs, rooftop parties, or lakeside chilling  
**Style:** Classic with a meme twist

**What you need:**
- A deck of cards
- A bottle of your favorite drink (or a mix—get creative)
- Shot glasses

**How to play:**
1. Shuffle the deck and spread the cards face down in a circle.
2. Each player takes turns drawing a card and matching it to a challenge (think Kings Cup, but with a Memorial Day spin).
3. Assign each card a dare—examples:
   - **2:** Give two sips.
   - **5:** Social (everyone drinks).
   - **7:** “Salute to the stars”—everyone stands and gives their wildest salute; the last person to do it drinks.
   - **10:** Meme round—show the funniest Memorial Day meme you can find. The group votes, and the loser drinks.
   - **Ace:** Make a rule (don’t say “BBQ” or you drink!).
4. Keep the game going as long as the cards last, or until the first player asks, “Wait, what day is it again?”

### 2. Grillmaster Dare Challenge

**Perfect for:** Grill-side action, backyard legends  
**Style:** Dare challenges with a foodie twist

**What you need:**
- A list of NSFW dares (keep it friendly for all, or get spicy if your group is up for it)
- A timer or phone
- Drinks

**How to play:**
1. Everyone sits in a circle. The Grillmaster (the person with the tongs) starts.
2. The Grillmaster picks a player and gives them a dare. If they complete it, the Grillmaster drinks. If they chicken out, they drink.
3. Rotate the Grillmaster after every round.
4. Dares can range from “Sing your country’s national anthem with a mouthful of chips” to “Try to convince everyone you’re a grill expert for the next round.”

### 3. Meme Drinking Game

**Perfect for:** Indoors, outdoors, or wherever Wi-Fi is strong  
**Style:** Viral drinking games meet meme culture

**What you need:**
- Phones with internet access
- Drinks

**How to play:**
1. Each round, pick a theme (like “best BBQ fail” or “most patriotic animal”).
2. Everyone has two minutes to find and share the most hilarious meme or video that fits.
3. The group votes for the top meme; all non-winners take a sip.
4. Bonus: If you’ve got meme drinking game packs from Drinking Dojo, throw them in for extra chaos.

### 4. Couples Flip Cup

**Perfect for:** Double dates, wedding party games, or couples drinking games  
**Style:** Classic drinking challenge with a team twist

**What you need:**
- Plastic cups (one per player)
- Table
- Drinks

**How to play:**
1. Teams of two line up on opposite sides of the table.
2. Each person drinks, then tries to flip their cup upside down on the table edge.
3. First team to finish wins; losers take a dare or finish their drinks.
4. Add in a Memorial Day twist: losers have to do their best Uncle Sam impression.
