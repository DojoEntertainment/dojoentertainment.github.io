## What Is the Nyt Connections Answers Drinking Game?

Imagine teaming up with your crew to crack word puzzles, but with a wild twist: every wrong answer means a sip, a dare, or a viral-worthy drinking challenge. This custom drinking game builder format is a hit for both puzzle nerds and daredevils, making it a favorite for everything from dorm room hangs to wedding party games.

## What You Need

- **A phone or laptop** (to pull up a recent Nyt Connections puzzle or create your own)
- **Drinks of choice** (beer, cocktails, mocktails—your dojo, your rules)
- **Friends** (2–10 players works best)
- **Dare cards or a list of NSFW dares** (optional, but guaranteed chaos)
- **Pen and paper** (if you want to keep score or track dares)

## How To Play

### 1. Set Up the Puzzle

- Head to the latest Nyt Connections puzzle online, or use a custom set of words from Drinking Dojo’s [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com).
- Display the words for everyone to see.

### 2. Form Teams

- Divide players into teams of 2–5.
- Each team takes turns trying to group the given words into sets based on their connections.

### 3. Rules

- **Right Connection:** If your team correctly groups four words, everyone else takes a sip!
- **Wrong Connection:** Miss the mark? Your team draws a dare card or chooses an NSFW dare from the list—then drinks.
- **No Guessing Out Loud:** Only one guess per turn. Any “helpful” hints from the sidelines = one sip for the peanut gallery.
- **Time Limit:** Set a timer (60 seconds per turn keeps it spicy).

### 4. Drinking Challenges and Dares

- Sprinkle in viral drinking games classics like “waterfall,” “never have I ever,” or meme-inspired challenges for bonus rounds.
- For extra chaos, add a Fortnite drinking game or election day drinking game twist: losing team does a viral dance, recites a meme, or answers a wild dare.

### 5. Winning the Night

- First team to solve the puzzle wins ultimate bragging rights and gets to assign a final round of dare challenges.

## Why This Game Slaps

- **Social AF:** Perfect for breaking the ice at parties, destination weddings, or couples drinking games nights.
- **Customizable:** Use your own inside jokes, memes, or themes—make it as tame or wild as your crew wants.
- **Global Vibes:** Whether you’re sipping lagers in Copenhagen or downing hard seltzers in Toronto, this party drinking game fits any house party.

## Pro Tips for Legendary Nights

- Rotate who leads each round for max chaos.
- Up the ante with regional themes (Inauguration Day drinking game for US friends, Eurovision for the UK and Denmark crowd).
- Keep the meme drinking games energy strong—award bonus points for funniest wrong answers.
