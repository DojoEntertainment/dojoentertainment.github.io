## Classic Eurovision Drinking Game Rules

Here’s a tried-and-true set of rules to keep the party going. Customize these with our [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) if you want to add your own memes or wild dares.

### Take A Sip When:
- The performer sings in a language other than English  
- There’s a key change mid-song  
- The camera zooms awkwardly on the crowd  
- Someone uses a wind machine or pyrotechnics  
- Hosts attempt a cringey joke  
- You spot a truly bizarre costume  
- There’s an obvious meme moment (bonus points for going viral in your group chat)  
- A country votes for its neighbor (looking at you, Scandinavia)  

### Finish Your Drink If:
- The UK gets *nul points*  
- There’s an on-stage technical fail  
- Someone actually falls off the stage  
- You correctly predict the winning country before votes start  

### Dare Challenges For The Brave

Keep your party spicy with dare challenges. Every time you land on a “dare square” (decide a trigger, like a country with a dramatic ballad), draw a dare card or use your phone to generate an NSFW dare. Think karaoke, TikTok dances, or letting the group post a meme-worthy pic of you.
