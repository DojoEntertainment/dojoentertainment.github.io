## Top Easter Drinking Games To Play This Year

### 1. **Egg Roulette**

Forget Russian Roulette — this one’s all about eggs and luck.

**What You Need:**
- 1 dozen eggs (half hard-boiled, half raw)
- Drinks of your choice

**How To Play:**
1. Mix the eggs up so nobody knows which are raw.
2. Players take turns picking an egg and smashing it on their forehead.
3. If you get a raw one, take a big drink. If you get hard-boiled, nominate someone else to drink.
4. Add a dare challenge (try one from [Drinking Dojo’s packs](https://drinkingdojo.com)) for extra chaos.

### 2. **Easter Meme Drinking Challenge**

Bring on the viral drinking games vibes with a meme twist.

**What You Need:**
- Pre-made meme cards (print or show on your phone), or use our custom drinking game builder to create your own meme challenges.

**How To Play:**
1. Lay out meme cards face down.
2. Players draw a card and must act out or caption the meme.
3. If the group laughs, everyone drinks. If it’s a flop, the person who drew the card drinks and takes a dare.
4. Mix in NSFW dares for adults-only fun.

### 3. **The Bunny Hop Dare Relay**

Perfect for couples drinking games or big groups.

**What You Need:**
- Bunny ears (DIY or store-bought)
- List of dares (get wild with NSFW dares or keep it tame)
- Drinks

**How To Play:**
1. Form teams or pairs.
2. First person wears bunny ears and completes a dare, then hops (literally) to the next teammate.
3. If you bail on the dare, finish your drink.
4. First team to finish all dares wins — losers take a group shot.

### 4. **Egg Hunt Drinking Edition**

Upgrade your classic house party ideas with an adult twist.

**What You Need:**
- Plastic eggs
- Slips of paper with dares, drink instructions, or meme challenges
- Drinks

**How To Play:**
1. Hide eggs around your party spot.
2. Players hunt for eggs — each egg contains a rule: drink, dare, or meme challenge.
3. The person with the most eggs hands out finishing drinks at the end.
