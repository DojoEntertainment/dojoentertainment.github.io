## How To Play the ESPN MLB Drinking Game

**Step 1: Set Up Teams**  
Divide your crew into two teams (or more if you’re feeling rowdy). Each team picks a side: Home or Away.

**Step 2: Assign Roles**  
Have one person serve as the “Umpire”—they keep an eye on the dares, check the rules, and call out drinking challenges throughout the game.

**Step 3: Play Ball with These Rules**

Every time one of these classic MLB moments happens during the ESPN broadcast, your squad drinks, dares, or both:

### Basic Rules

- **Home Run:** Everyone takes two sips. Last to cheer does a dare.
- **Pitching Change:** Chug for 5 seconds.
- **Strikeout:** Batter’s team drinks.
- **Walk:** Opposing team drinks.
- **Double Play:** Both teams swap drinks for one round.
- **Meme Moment (awkward fan, wild mascot, etc):** Umpire picks someone for an NSFW dare or meme drinking game move.
- **Commercial Break:** Everyone completes a quick dare challenge before the ads are over.

### Bonus Dare Challenges

- **Fortnite Victory Dance:** If a player scores, someone on the losing team must do their best Fortnite dance—if they refuse, they drink.
- **Wedding Party Toast:** If there’s a proposal on screen (it happens!), everyone gives a toast and drinks.
- **Couples Drinking Games Twist:** If you’re playing as couples, swap drinks with your partner every time the bases are loaded.

## Winning and House Rules

- **Game ends at the final pitch or when everyone agrees they’ve had enough fun (or drinks).**
- **Umpire’s decisions are final. If you argue a call, prepare for a dare challenge.**
