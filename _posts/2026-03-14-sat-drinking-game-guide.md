## What Is the Sat Drinking Game?

Sat is the super-social, meme-fueled drinking game that’s blowing up on TikTok and group chats everywhere. The idea? Keep the drinks (and dares) flowing, but with a twist that keeps everyone guessing and laughing. It’s perfect for chill nights, rowdy house parties, or even as a couples drinking game if you’re feeling brave.

## How To Play Sat

### Setup

1. Everyone grabs a drink and sits in a circle.
2. Shuffle the deck of cards and place it in the center.
3. Nominate a Sat Master for the first round (you can pick based on who arrived last, has the best meme knowledge, or just volunteer).

### Rules

- The Sat Master draws a card and reads out the suit and number.
- Each suit has a different challenge or rule:
  - **Hearts**: Dare challenge! The Sat Master picks someone to do a dare (get creative — or use the meme drinking games pack for ideas).
  - **Spades**: Sat round! All players must say “Sat” instead of any other word for the next three turns, or take a sip every time they mess up.
  - **Diamonds**: Drinking challenge! The person who drew the card chooses a drinking challenge for someone (think Fortnite drinking game rules or classic party drinking games like flip cup).
  - **Clubs**: Meme moment! The Sat Master shows a random meme (from their phone or a meme pack), and everyone votes on who in the group it fits best. That person drinks.
- Number cards mean sips — take as many sips as the number drawn.
- Face cards activate a group dare: everyone participates in a viral drinking game mini-challenge (like waterfall, categories, or a quick-fire question round).

### How To Win

The Sat Drinking Game isn’t really about “winning” — it’s about surviving the most dare challenges, avoiding epic fails, and racking up hilarious stories for the next day. Play until the deck runs out or your group’s ready for the next round of house party ideas.
