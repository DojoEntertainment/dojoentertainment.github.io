- Your favorite drinks (beer, cocktails, seltzers, you name it)  
- Scorecards or paper and pens  
- Dare cards or a custom drinking game builder (check out [Drinking Dojo’s custom tools](https://drinkingdojo.com))

### How To Play The PGA Tour Drinking Game

#### The Basics

1. **Pick Your Players:** Gather your crew—this works for couples drinking games or big group house party ideas.
2. **Choose Your Event:** Turn on a real PGA Tour broadcast, or play a highlight reel if live coverage isn’t available.
3. **Grab Your Drinks:** Everyone should have their beverage of choice within reach.
4. **Set Up Dare Challenges:** Write down some NSFW dares or meme drinking games twists to spice things up.

#### The Rules

Every time one of these happens during the PGA Tour, everyone drinks:

- **Birdie:** Take 1 sip.
- **Eagle or Better:** Everyone finishes their drink.
- **Bogey:** Last person to yell “FORE!” takes a dare.
- **Player Misses a Short Putt:** 2 sips.
- **Water Hazard or Sand Trap:** Make a toast to the golfer’s “skills” and take a group drink.
- **Commentator Mentions a Player’s Outfit:** Give a drink to someone wearing the same color.
- **Crowd Cheers Wildly:** Start a “golf clap” (soft clapping), last one to join takes 2 sips.

##### Bonus Dare Challenges

Pull a dare card or use the custom drinking game builder for these spicy extras:

- **If a player shanks a shot,** do a meme-worthy dance (Fortnite moves, anyone?) or take a dare.
- **If someone says “Tiger Woods,”** choose someone to take a shot or pull a NSFW dare.
- **If a player sinks a putt over 20 feet,** everyone does their best golf swing and drinks.

### Pro Tips For Legendary PGA Tour Parties

- **Wedding party games:** Perfect for the sports-loving couple and their guests.
- **Add some house party ideas:** Set up a mini-putt station for bonus drinking challenges.
- **Go international:** Works great across the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, and Denmark—just swap in local drink favorites.
