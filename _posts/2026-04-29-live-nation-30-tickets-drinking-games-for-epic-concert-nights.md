### Game 1: Setlist Showdown — A Wedding Party Game & House Party Idea

Perfect for any concert — whether it’s a wedding party game or a wild house party idea before heading out.

**How to Play:**
1. **Pick Your Artist:** Everyone chooses a song they think will be on the artist’s setlist tonight.
2. **Make Your Picks:** Write down your predictions (pen and paper or notes app).
3. **The Rules:**
   - If your song gets played, assign a drink to someone else.

**Materials Needed:** Drinks, a way to keep score, and the setlist (check online during the show).
