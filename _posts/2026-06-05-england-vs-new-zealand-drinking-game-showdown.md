### Game Rules: England vs New Zealand Edition

This drinking game brings together the best of party drinking games and drinking challenges, with rounds designed for every match highlight, meme moment, and wild play. Here’s how to play:

#### 1. **Pick Your Side**

- Split into two teams: Team England and Team New Zealand.
- Each team picks a signature toast (bonus points for accents and banter).

#### 2. **Match Moments = Drink Time**

Take a sip or complete a dare whenever these match events happen:

- **Score Change:** Every time your team scores, the other team drinks.
- **Epic Fail:** Any dropped catch, missed goal, or obvious blunder? Everyone takes a drink.
- **Commentator Cliché:** When you hear classic phrases like "he gave it 110%" or "that’s a howler," the first person to shout “meme game!” chooses who drinks.
- **Penalty or Foul:** The player who last committed a party foul (spilled a drink, forgot a rule, etc.) does a dare challenge.

#### 3. **NSFW Dares & Meme Rounds**

- At halftime or between periods, whip out the NSFW dares for those feeling brave (keep it legal and consensual, obviously).
- Losers of each round must perform a meme-worthy dare—think TikTok dance, dramatic Haka imitation, or the worst British accent you can muster.

#### 4. **Bonus Rounds**

- **Sudden Death Sip:** If the score is tied at the end, everyone races to finish their drink—last one to finish does a dare picked by the group.
- **House Party Ideas:** For bigger groups, assign secret “keywords” (like “rugby,” “cricket,” “All Blacks,” or “Three Lions”). If someone says a keyword, they drink and pick a mate to join them.
