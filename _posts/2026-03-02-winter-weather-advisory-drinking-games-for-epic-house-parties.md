## Top Drinking Games for a Winter Weather Advisory

Ready to make the most of your next housebound hangout? Here are our top picks for drinking games guaranteed to turn a snowed-in night into a wild one.

### 1. **Blizzard Blitz**

**Materials:**  
- Deck of cards  
- Your favorite drinks  
- Dare list (check Drinking Dojo for wild NSFW dares or keep it tame)

**How to Play:**  
- Shuffle the cards and spread them out face down.  
- Each player draws a card on their turn.  
- Each suit is a different mini-challenge:  
  - **Hearts:** Give out drinks.  
  - **Diamonds:** Take a sip.  
  - **Clubs:** Do a dare.  
  - **Spades:** Make a rule (no swearing, no using first names, etc.).  
- Jokers mean everyone drinks!

**Why it rocks:** Every round is unpredictable, just like the weather outside.

### 2. **Snowed-In Meme Showdown**

**Materials:**  
- Your phones  
- Drinking Dojo meme drinking games pack (or create your own with the custom drinking game builder)  
- Drinks

**How to Play:**  
- One player shows a meme (from Drinking Dojo’s pack or their own collection).  
- Everyone else reacts with their best meme face.  
- The person showing the meme picks the best reaction—winner gives out two drinks, loser takes two.  
- Add in dare challenges for extra chaos.

**Why it rocks:** It’s the ultimate blend of meme culture and party drinking games.

### 3. **Igloo Dare Challenges**

**Materials:**  
- Printed dare challenges (check out Drinking Dojo’s list for inspiration)  
- Drinks

**How to Play:**  
- Write down a bunch of wild or NSFW dares, fold them up, and drop them in a bowl.  
- Players pick a dare on their turn.  
- Complete the dare or take two sips.  
- Up the ante by adding couples drinking games dares for pairs!

**Why it rocks:** You’ll discover who’s brave enough to handle the coldest dares.
