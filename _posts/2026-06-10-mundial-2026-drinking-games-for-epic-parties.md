### What You’ll Need

- A TV or streaming device for the match
- Your favorite drinks (beer, cocktails, mocktails—your call)
- A group of friends (the more, the better)
- Custom drinking game builder from [Drinking Dojo](https://drinkingdojo.com) (optional, but highly recommended)
- Printed or digital dare cards (bonus points for meme drinking games)

### Basic Rules

1. **Pick Your Teams**  
   Split the group into two: each side roots for one of the teams playing. If you’re a mixed crowd, randomize team assignments with a coin toss.

2. **Set Up Dare Challenges**  
   Prepare a stack of dares—think NSFW dares for the brave, or meme-inspired challenges for the jokers. Use our custom drinking game builder to personalize the dares.

3. **Drink Triggers**  
   Every time one of these events happens, everyone drinks (or completes a dare):
   - Yellow card: 1 sip
   - Red card: 2 sips
   - Goal: Everyone on the conceding team drinks
   - Offside: Last person to shout “offside!” drinks
   - Missed penalty: Everyone does a dare challenge
   - Announcer says “historic moment”: Group cheers and drinks
   - Epic save: Do a dancing dare

4. **Bonus Meme Round**  
   Use meme drinking games packs to spice it up—think viral TikTok challenges, or cards based on the funniest mundial 2026 moments.

### How To Win

The real winners are those with the best stories (and maybe the fewest regrets). But if you want to keep score:
- The team with the most goals chooses a dare for the losers at the end.
- Or, keep it casual and play until the final whistle, then vote for the MVP of the game (Most Valuable Partier).
