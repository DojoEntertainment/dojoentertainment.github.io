## How to Play: Stranger Things Edition

### Basic Rules

1. **Pick Your Episode(s):**  
   Choose a season or shuffle through your favorite episodes.

2. **Assign Characters:**  
   Everyone picks a main character. Whenever your character appears or does something iconic, you drink.

3. **Take a Sip When:**
   - The lights flicker
   - Someone says “Upside Down”
   - Eleven eats waffles
   - Hopper drinks coffee or beer
   - Any Demogorgon appears
   - Classic 80s music plays
   - Dustin drops a science fact
   - A walkie-talkie crackles

4. **Dare Challenges:**  
   Whenever someone misses a cue or refuses to drink, pull a dare card. These can range from “Speak in Demogorgon growls for the next round” to spicy NSFW dares, depending on your group’s mood.

5. **Shot Rounds:**  
   If a major plot twist happens (someone disappears, a monster attack, or a character cries), everyone takes a shot.
