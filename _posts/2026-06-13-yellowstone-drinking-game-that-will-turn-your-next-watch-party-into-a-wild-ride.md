If you’re obsessed with Yellowstone and looking for new drinking games to spice up your next binge session, saddle up. Whether you’re pregaming with friends or throwing a rowdy house party, a Yellowstone-themed party drinking game is the perfect way to bring some Dutton-level drama to your night. From meme-worthy one-liners to iconic standoffs, this game is loaded with dare challenges and custom rules that’ll make every episode unforgettable.

## What You’ll Need

- Your favorite drinks (beer, seltzer, cocktails, or non-alcoholic for the rebels)
- A screen streaming Yellowstone (any season works—trust us, chaos is guaranteed)
- Dare challenges printed or loaded on your phone (check out our [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) for wild ideas)
- A group of 2+ friends who aren’t afraid of a little mayhem

## How To Play The Yellowstone Drinking Game

**Set the scene:** Get comfy, grab your drinks, and make sure everyone knows the rules. Assign someone as the “Ranch Boss” to keep things on track (or let chaos reign, your call).

### Basic Rules

Take a sip when:
- John Dutton says something that could make a cowboy cry
- Beth drops a savage insult
- Rip does something that would get you fired in real life
- Someone orders whiskey, shotguns a beer, or pours a drink
- There’s a flashback scene

Take two sips when:
- A fight breaks out (verbal or physical)
- Someone threatens to leave the ranch—again
- There’s a scene at the train station (you know the one)

Finish your drink or do a dare when:
- Someone gets branded
- There’s a dramatic horse chase
- Beth throws a drink (bonus points if you can keep up)

Add a twist with **NSFW dares**: If you want to level up, toss in some NSFW dares every time a character takes things *way too far* (which is basically every episode). Use our [dare challenges](https://drinkingdojo.com) or create your own with our custom game builder.

## Customizing Your Yellowstone Party

Want to go viral? Snap memes of your wildest moments and tag your crew. Bonus points for cowboy hats, flannel, and fake mustaches. For couples drinking games or wedding party games, throw in romantic dares every time Kayce and Monica have a moment.

Looking for more house party ideas? Mix Yellowstone with other viral drinking games or try a Fortnite drinking game between episodes for maximum hype.

## Tips For A Legendary Night

- **Pace yourself:** The Duttons may be wild, but your liver isn’t bulletproof.
- **Stay hydrated:** Have water on hand—you’ll thank us later.
- **Keep it social:** The more friends, the more chaos (and the better the memes).
