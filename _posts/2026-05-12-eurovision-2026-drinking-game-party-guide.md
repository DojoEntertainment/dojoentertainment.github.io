### Basic Rules for Eurovision Drinking Games

1. **Pick Your Country**  
   Each player (or team) chooses a participating country to rep for the night. Whenever your country is mentioned or performs, take a drink.

2. **Drink On These Eurovision Classics**  
   - Costume change mid-song: 1 drink  
   - Wind machine or pyrotechnics: 1 drink  
   - Lyrics about love or unity: 1 drink  
   - Awkward presenter banter: 1 drink  
   - Meme-worthy dance moves: 2 drinks  
   - Key change so dramatic your nan would gasp: 2 drinks  
   - Judges give 12 points to the same country multiple times: Finish your drink  

3. **Meme Drinking Games Challenge**  
   - Someone in the group spots a viral moment (think: epic fail, accidental meme, or legendary outfit). First to call it out assigns a dare challenge to anyone at the table.

4. **NSFW Dare Round**  
   - At halftime, each player writes down an NSFW dare (keep it safe and consensual, folks). Put them in a hat. If your country finishes in the bottom five, you pick a random dare.
