## Why Excessive Heat Calls for Cool Drinking Games

When the weather’s scorching, you need more than a lukewarm playlist. You need *ice-breakers* that double as drinking challenges, with rules simple enough to remember after your third round. Whether it's a house party, backyard BBQ, or wedding rager, a little excessive heat is just an excuse to get creative—with your drinks and your dares.

## Game 1: Icebreaker Inferno

### What You Need:
- A bag of ice cubes
- Your favorite drinks
- Friends who don’t melt under pressure

### How to Play:
1. Everyone grabs an ice cube and holds it in their hand.
2. The host (or meme overlord) fires off a dare challenge from the [Drinking Dojo dare generator](https://drinkingdojo.com).
3. Complete the dare *while holding your ice cube*. Drop it? Take a big sip.
4. Last person with their ice still intact wins and can assign a dare to anyone.

**Why it works:** The ice keeps your hands cool, and the dares keep the energy hotter than the sun.

## Game 2: Meme Meltdown

### What You Need:
- A deck of meme drinking game cards (DIY or from Drinking Dojo)
- Chilled drinks
- A sense of humor

### Rules:
1. Draw a meme card on your turn and read it aloud.
2. If you relate to the meme, drink.
3. If *everyone* relates? It’s a waterfall—keep drinking until the person to your right stops.
4. For an extra challenge: Snap a selfie reenacting the meme. Funniest pic gets to skip their next drink.

**Pro tip:** This game is perfect for those viral drinking games you see all over TikTok and Instagram.

## Game 3: Sunburn Dare Roulette

### What You Need:
- A spinning app or spinning wheel
- List of NSFW dares or regular dares, your pick
- Cold beverages

### How to Play:
1. Spin to see who’s up.
2. That person chooses: attempt the dare, or take two sips.
3. Include some *excessive heat* themed dares, like “Chug an ice-cold drink in 10 seconds” or “Do your best sun dance.”

**Spice it up:** Use the [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) to create your own set of wild, summer-ready dares.

## Game 4: Poolside Pints

### Materials:
- Inflatable pool (optional, but epic)
- Floating drink holders
- Any drink you want

### How to Play:
1. Everyone floats (or lounges) with a drink in hand.
2. Set a timer. Every five minutes, the host yells out a random challenge—like “last one to splash takes a sip” or “best cannonball wins immunity.”
3. For extra laughs, throw in wedding party games or couples drinking games if your crowd is up for it.

## House Party Ideas for Surviving the Heat

- **Frozen Shot Glasses:** Freeze juice or mixers in silicone molds for instant chill.
- **Drinking Challenges:** Who can finish their drink without their ice melting? Loser takes a dare.
- **Election Day or Inauguration Day Drinking Game:** Turn any live event into a party with custom rules for every buzzword or wild moment.

## Safety Tips for Heatwave Parties

- Stay hydrated with water breaks.
- Use sunscreen if you’re outside.
- Know your limits—excessive heat and excessive drinking don’t mix for everyone.
