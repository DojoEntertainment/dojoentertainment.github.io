## Turn World Cup Results Into Unforgettable Drinking Games

Let’s be real: watching the world cup is only half the fun. The other half? Turning every goal, penalty, and meme-worthy moment into a reason to raise your glass. Whether you’re planning a house party, pregaming before the next match, or just vibing with your crew, these party drinking games are guaranteed to keep the energy high.

### 1. **Score Sips**

Every goal changes the game, so make it change yours too!  
**How to play:**
- **Everyone picks a team** before kickoff.  
- **Whenever your team scores**, everyone else takes 2 sips.  
- If your team *concedes*, you knock back 3 sips.  
- **Bonus:** If the game's a draw at full time, everyone does a dare challenge (see below).

**Materials:** Drinks of choice, a list of dares (or use our [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) to generate spicy dares automatically).

### 2. **Commentator Chaos**

Ever noticed how wild commentators get during world cup matches? Time to make them part of your drinking game.  
**How to play:**
- Each player picks a classic commentator phrase (like “unbelievable!” or “what a strike!”).
- Every time your phrase is used, *everyone else* drinks.
- If someone forgets to drink, they face an NSFW dare or meme drinking game challenge.

**Materials:** TV or stream, drinks, list of phrases.

### 3. **Half-Time Dare Challenges**

Halftime is usually for snacks, but why not spice it up with viral drinking games and dare challenges?  
**How to play:**
- Write down dares (think NSFW dares or meme-inspired challenges) on slips of paper.
- At halftime, everyone draws a dare and has to complete it or drink.
- Up the stakes by letting the group create custom dares using our [drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com).

**Materials:** Paper, pens, drinks, your wildest ideas.

### 4. **Penalty Shot Showdown**

Those nerve-wracking penalty shootouts can make or break a match—and your sobriety.  
**How to play:**
- Each missed penalty = take a shot.
- Each penalty scored by your team = assign a shot to someone else.
- If the shootout goes to sudden death, everyone drinks together for solidarity.

**Materials:** Shot glasses, your favorite spirits.
