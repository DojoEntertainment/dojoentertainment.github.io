## Top Influencer-Approved Drinking Games To Try

### 1. Meme Drinking Game

**Materials Needed:**  
- A pack of our [meme drinking games](https://drinkingdojo.com)  
- Your favorite drinks  
- A group ready to laugh  

**How To Play:**  
- Shuffle the meme cards and place them in the center.  
- Each player draws a card and reads it out loud.  
- Follow the instructions—whether it’s acting out a viral meme, completing a dare challenge, or nominating someone to drink.  
- The wilder the interpretation, the better—don’t forget to film those hilarious moments for your own socials.  

**Why It’s Viral:**  
Influencers love memes, and these games are packed with references that’ll get everyone in stitches. Plus, they’re perfect for making TikTok-worthy content.

### 2. Dare Challenge Roulette

**Materials Needed:**  
- Dare challenge cards (DIY or from our custom drinking game builder)  
- A spinning app or bottle  

**How To Play:**  
- Place all dare cards in a stack.  
- Players spin the bottle. Whoever it lands on must pick the top dare and do it—or take a drink.  
- Challenges range from NSFW dares (for the brave) to classic goofball antics.  

**Why It’s Viral:**  
Influencers are all about escalating dares and reactions—think of this as your chance to become the next trending drinking challenge.

### 3. Themed Drinking Game Nights

**Materials Needed:**  
- Themed packs like the Fortnite drinking game or wedding party games  
- Drinks and snacks  
- Props (optional for extra flair)  

**How To Play:**  
- Choose your theme—maybe it’s a Fortnite drinking game, or a custom inauguration day drinking game.  
- Divide into teams or play as individuals.  
- Complete challenges, answer trivia, or survive elimination rounds—last one standing wins.  

**Why It’s Viral:**  
Themes make it easy to tie your drinking games to whatever’s hot online, from election day drinking games to couples drinking games for date night.

- **Stay safe:** Always know your limits and keep things fun, not reckless.
