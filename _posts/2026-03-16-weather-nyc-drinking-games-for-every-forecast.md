## NYC Weather Watch Drinking Game

**Ready to make meteorology fun?**

This drinking game is perfect when you’re watching the weather channel, checking your app, or just staring out the window wishing for sun. It’s easy to play, works with any group, and needs minimal setup.

### What You’ll Need

- Any drink of choice (beer, seltzer, cocktails—whatever’s in the fridge)
- Access to a NYC weather forecast (TV, phone app, or radio)
- A sense of humor

### How To Play

1. **Set Up:** Everyone grabs a drink and tunes in to the latest NYC weather update.
2. **Choose Your Triggers:** Before you start, agree on the weather-related words or events that will trigger drinks. For example:
   - *Take a sip every time you hear “unpredictable” or “rollercoaster.”*
   - *Drink every time the forecaster says “chance of showers.”*
   - *Finish your drink if a weather alert pops up or the reporter talks about subway delays “due to weather conditions.”*
3. **Bonus Dare Challenges:** Every time the temperature drops or rises by more than 10 degrees in the same segment, the group comes up with a wild dare for the person who predicted the wrong temp.

### Optional Meme Drinking Games Twist

Add some viral drinking games flavor by creating memes about the forecast (“NYC in spring: snow boots and shorts in the same week”). Anyone who laughs at a meme has to drink!

## Rainy Day Dare Challenge

Stuck inside because NYC’s turned into a rain forest? Time for some NSFW dares and ridiculous fun.

### What You’ll Need

- Custom dare cards (make your own or use our [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com))
- Drinks for everyone

### How To Play

1. **Deck Prep:** Write dares on slips of paper or use the Drinking Dojo builder to shuffle in some spicy, meme drinking games-approved challenges.
2. **Take Turns:** Each round, one player draws a dare. If they complete it, everyone else drinks. If they chicken out, they drink double!
3. **Weather Bonus:** If thunder or lightning happens during the game, everyone has to finish what’s left in their glass.

## NYC Rooftop Forecast Game

Summer in NYC means rooftop parties and sunset vibes. Here’s how to make it legendary:

### What You’ll Need

- A rooftop (or balcony, or fire escape—NYC style)
- Your favorite party drinking games pack
- Drinks on ice

### How To Play

1. **Forecast Bets:** At the start, everyone guesses the temperature at sunset. Closest guess picks the first drinking challenge.
2. **Wind Watch:** Every time a gust of wind knocks over a plastic cup or napkin, the last person to notice has to do a dare.
3. **Sunset Cheers:** When the sun finally sets, everyone raises a glass and downs their drink to “NYC weather, always keeping us on our toes.”
