## NBA Playoff Picture Drinking Game Rules

Ready to raise the stakes? Here’s how to bring the playoff action to your living room.

### What You Need

- A screen to stream the NBA playoff games  
- Your favorite drinks (beer, cocktails, seltzers—whatever fits the vibe)  
- A group of friends (2+ players for max fun)  
- Printed or digital list of challenges and dares  
- Optional: Custom drinking game builder from [Drinking Dojo](https://drinkingdojo.com) for extra wild rounds

### How to Play

1. **Set Up Teams**  
   Split into two teams (East vs West, or by favorite players). If you’re feeling chaotic, randomise teams every quarter.

2. **Game Triggers**  
   Take a sip, shot, or complete a dare every time one of these happens:
   - **Three-pointer made:** Everyone drinks.
   - **Timeout called:** Last person to yell “timeout” drinks.
   - **Technical foul:** Pick someone to do a dare challenge.
   - **Dunk:** Next player must meme a commentator’s reaction or drink.
   - **Missed free throw:** Team of the player who missed drinks.
   - **Overtime:** Everyone finishes their drink.

3. **NSFW Dares and Meme Challenges**  
   Spice it up with custom dares. Use the Drinking Dojo custom drinking game builder to create wild, meme-worthy challenges for special moments—like a game-winning shot or a player flopping so hard it goes viral.

4. **Wildcard Rounds**  
   At halftime, draw a card or use your phone to generate a random dare. It could be anything from “Fortnite dance-off challenge” (hello, fortnite drinking game crossover) to “recite your best commentator impersonation or take two sips.”
