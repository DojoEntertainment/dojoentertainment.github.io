## How To Play Celtic vs Rangers Drinking Games

This party drinking game is perfect for groups—split everyone into two teams: Celtic supporters and Rangers supporters (pick your side, or let a coin flip decide).

### Basic Rules

1. **Pre-Game Toast**  
   Each team does a group shot to kick things off. Losers of rock-paper-scissors do an extra dare.

2. **Drink for Every Goal Scored**  
   If your team scores, the other team drinks. If it’s a penalty, both teams drink.

3. **Yellow Cards Rule**  
   When a referee hands out a yellow card, everyone on the offending team does a sip. Add a dare challenge if it’s two yellows in a row.

4. **Red Cards Rule**  
   The whole room drinks, and the team with the red card pulls an NSFW dare from the deck.

5. **Commentator Clichés Challenge**  
   Every time the commentator uses a classic line (like “Old Firm rivalry” or “brilliant finish”), last person to raise their glass does a shot.

6. **Halftime Meme Break**  
   At halftime, pause for a meme drinking game: share the funniest football meme you can find—loser takes a drink and a dare.

7. **Full-Time Forfeit**  
   Losing team does a group dare (bonus points for wedding party games or couples drinking games twists if you’re feeling spicy).
