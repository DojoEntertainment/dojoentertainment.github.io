### Must-Try Drinking Games for Snow Days

#### 1. **Snowed-In Showdown**

Perfect for groups who love a little friendly competition.

**What you need:**
- A deck of cards
- Drinks of choice
- Post-its or small pieces of paper for dares

**How to play:**
1. Everyone sits in a circle. Deal 5 cards to each player.
2. On your turn, play a card and challenge another player—either to drink, complete a dare, or both.
3. If you play the same number as someone before you, double the challenge.
4. Run out of cards? You’re safe! Last one with cards has to do an NSFW dare (if everyone’s cool with it) or finish their drink.

#### 2. **Snowflake Dare Challenges**

Time to get creative with those dare challenges!

**What you need:**
- Slips of paper
- A bowl
- Drinks

**How to play:**
1. Before starting, everyone writes out a few dares—funny, flirty, or full-on meme drinking game chaos.
2. Mix them up in a bowl.
3. Take turns drawing a dare. Complete it or take two sips.
4. Bonus: If you’re feeling spicy, use our [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) to create a snow day-themed pack on the spot!

#### 3. **Blizzard Battle Royale**

Inspired by the Fortnite drinking game, but no gaming skills required.

**What you need:**
- Two teams
- A timer
- Drinks

**How to play:**
1. Split into teams. Each team picks a “champion.”
2. Set a timer for one minute. Champions face off in a silly snow-inspired challenge (think: fastest to build a mini snow fort out of couch cushions).
3. Losing team drinks.
4. Rotate champions and keep the blizzard battles going.

#### 4. **Couples Cabin Fever**

Great for couples or pairs who want to heat things up.

**What you need:**
- A list of flirty or cheeky challenges
- Drinks

**How to play:**
1. Take turns picking a challenge from the list.
2. Complete the task or sip.
3. Up the ante with some NSFW dares if you’re feeling bold.
