## How To Play: Wimbledon 2026 Drinking Game Rules & Dare Challenges

### Basic Rules

Get everyone together and explain the rules before the match starts. Decide if you want to play singles (everyone for themselves) or doubles (teams). Make sure everyone’s got their drink ready—then let the games begin.

### Take A Sip When:
- The umpire shushes the crowd
- A player challenges a call
- There’s a slow-motion replay of an epic point
- The camera zooms in on strawberries and cream
- Someone in the room tries to pronounce a player’s name and fails

### Take Two Sips When:
- A player serves an ace
- A player slips or falls
- The commentators mention “pressure”
- The match gets interrupted by rain (bonus if you’re outside and it starts raining for real)

### Dare Challenges To Amp Up The Fun

Ready to raise the stakes? Try these dare challenges:

- **Meme Drinking Game:** The first person who spots a meme-worthy reaction from the crowd or a player gets to assign a dare (like “speak in a British accent until the next point”).
- **NSFW Dares:** Winner of each set gets to pick an NSFW dare for someone else. Keep it fun, keep it consensual!
- **Viral Drinking Games:** If a streaker or wild fan shows up on camera (hey, it’s Wimbledon), everyone finishes their drink.

### Final Set Showdown

For the final set, up the ante—every double fault means everyone drinks, and the next person to guess the winner wrong has to do a group dare.
