## How to Play: Champions League Draw Drinking Game

### 1. **Pre-Game Setup**
- Gather your squad (2+ players; bigger groups = bigger chaos).
- Everyone picks a team from the draw—if your team is called out, you’re in for a challenge.
- Set up a list of dare challenges or use our custom dare generator for max meme potential.

### 2. **The Rules**

**When your team is drawn:**  
- Take two sips for the first round.
- Take a shot if your team lands in the “group of death.”

**If your rival team is drawn:**  
- Dole out a dare challenge to any player.

**Wildcard moments:**  
- *If a team from your country is picked:* Everyone from that country drinks.
- *Legendary memes or awkward moments from hosts/presenters:* Everyone drinks and shouts “VAR!”

**Couples drinking games twist:**  
- If you’re playing as a duo, each time your team gets a “tough” group, switch drinks with your partner.

### 3. **Dare Challenges**

Ready to go viral? Add these meme drinking games dares:
- Post a selfie with your least-favorite team’s scarf.
- Try a TikTok dance celebrating the “underdog” team.
