### Michigan Football Drinking Games: The Rules

Mix and match these rules or play them all for the wildest game day yet. These drinking challenges are perfect for both college dorms and wedding party games, so everyone can hop in.

#### 1. **First Down Frenzy**

- Every time Michigan gets a first down, everyone takes a sip.
- If it’s a huge run or pass, add an extra sip for the hype.

#### 2. **Touchdown Toast**

- When Michigan scores a touchdown, everyone cheers and finishes their drink.
- Opponent touchdown? Take a shot (or a big gulp).

#### 3. **Flag On The Play**

- Ref throws a flag? The last person to shout “Go Blue!” does a dare.
- Use our custom drinking game builder to create wild NSFW dares or meme-inspired challenges.

#### 4. **Announcer Bingo**

- Make bingo cards with classic football clichés (“That’s a huge play,” “He wants that one back,” etc.).
- When you get a line, everyone else drinks, and you get to pick a dare for the group.

#### 5. **Mascot Madness**

- Every time the Michigan mascot appears on screen, everyone does a group cheers and drinks.

### Bonus Dare Challenges

- **Fortnite Celebration:** If a player does a Fortnite-style dance after a touchdown, everyone does a group dare.
- **Meme Drinking Games:** Add meme dares for viral moments (“If Harbaugh takes off his hat, everyone must attempt their best coach impression and drink”).

### How To Play

1. Gather your crew and divide up the drinks.
2. Decide which rules and dares you want to include — tweak with the [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) for extra spice.
3. Assign someone as the “referee” to keep everyone honest and hand out dares.
4. Start the game, keep the drinks flowing, and the meme drinking games rolling.
5. Most importantly: keep it safe, know your limits, and make sure everyone has a good time.
