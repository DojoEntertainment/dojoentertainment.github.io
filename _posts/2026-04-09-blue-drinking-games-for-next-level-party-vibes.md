## The Blue Cup Challenge

This is the ultimate viral drinking game, perfect for groups who love a good dare challenge mixed with a bit of luck.

### How To Play

1. **Setup:**  
   Fill several party cups with blue-colored drinks. Make sure one cup contains something completely different (like water, soda, or a super-strong mix — your choice!).

2. **The Spin:**  
   Place all cups in a circle. The first player spins a bottle in the center.

3. **Drink or Dare:**  
   Whoever the bottle points to picks a cup and drinks it. If they get the odd one out, they have to complete a dare challenge chosen by the group (think NSFW dares, meme-inspired craziness, or anything that fits your party vibe).

4. **Repeat:**  
   Keep spinning, sipping, and daring until everyone’s had their moment of glory (or embarrassment).

## Blue Meme Drinking Game

If you’re a fan of meme drinking games, this one’s for you. It’s simple, wild, and always gets people talking.

### How To Play

1. **Collect Blue Memes:**  
   Have everyone submit their favorite blue-themed memes onto their phones.

2. **Show and Tell:**  
   Take turns showing a meme. If the group laughs, the meme owner takes a drink. If nobody laughs, the owner chooses someone else to drink.

3. **Dare Twist:**  
   For bonus chaos, whoever brings the least funny meme (decided by a group vote) has to complete a dare challenge at the end of the round.

## Blue Couples Drinking Games

Wanna spice things up with your partner? Try this easy blue couples game.

### How To Play

1. **Truth or Blue:**  
   Each person asks their partner a question. If you answer honestly, you’re safe. If you refuse, you must sip from a blue drink.

2. **Dare Option:**  
   Add in blue-themed dares for when you both want to take things up a notch.

## Wedding Party Blue Challenge

For those destination weddings or afterparties, the wedding party games don’t have to be prim and proper. Bring out the blue!

### How To Play

1. **Blue Shots Relay:**  
   Split into teams. Each team member has to finish a blue shot before tagging in the next. First team to finish wins — the slowest team does a dare challenge picked by the winners.
