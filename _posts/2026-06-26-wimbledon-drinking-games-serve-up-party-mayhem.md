## Wimbledon Drinking Game Rules

### The Classic Sips

Keep these rules on hand for every match. Remember, drink responsibly and pace yourself—Wimbledon is a marathon, not a sprint!

- **Every Ace:** Everyone drinks.
- **Double Fault:** Last person to shout “Fault!” does a dare.
- **Player Tantrum (racquet smash, loud complain):** Finish your drink.
- **Hawk-Eye Challenge:** If a player challenges a call, first person to shout “Out!” gives out three drinks.
- **Strawberries and Cream Mentioned:** Group toast!
- **Tie Breaker:** Everyone drinks twice.

### The Dare Rally

Ready to raise the stakes? Level up with these dare challenges—just grab a tennis ball and let fate decide.

- When the umpire says “Let,” the person holding the tennis ball must do an NSFW dare (keep it party-appropriate, or go wild if it’s just your crew).
- If the camera shows a celebrity in the crowd, first to call out their name picks someone for a dare challenge.
- Match commentary gets spicy? Invent a meme drinking game on the spot (e.g., “Drink every time Federer is called a legend”).

### Meme Mashup

Wimbledon is meme central—fancy a viral drinking game?

- Every time the crowd does a wave, drink and post your best Wimbledon meme to your group chat.
- Whenever someone mentions “underdog,” everyone shares their favorite tennis meme—loser drinks.
- If a player’s outfit becomes a meme on Twitter, everyone finishes their drink.
