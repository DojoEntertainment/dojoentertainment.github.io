## Boston Marathon 2026 Drinking Game Rules

### 1. **Runner’s Sip**

Every time the TV commentator mentions a famous runner or highlights a new country, everyone takes a sip. Want to go harder? Take two sips if it’s a runner from your country!

### 2. **Heartbreak Hill Challenge**

Whenever Heartbreak Hill is mentioned or shown, last person to shout “Push through!” does a dare challenge (think: sing the Boston Red Sox anthem, or send a meme in the group chat chosen by the group). Perfect for meme drinking games fans!

### 3. **Finish Line Frenzy**

When the first runner crosses the finish line, everyone finishes their drink. Last one done gets an NSFW dare (optional, depending on your party vibe).

### 4. **Hydration Hero**

If you spot a runner grabbing a water cup, EVERYONE drinks water (hydrate or diedrate, folks). Bonus: The next person to forget has to take a penalty sip.

### 5. **Pace Breaker**

Set a timer for every 15 minutes. When it goes off, do a quick dare challenge — like a plank, dance-off, or say your wildest marathon-inspired toast.
