## Weather or Not: The Ultimate Louisville Drinking Game

### What You’ll Need
- Your favorite party crew (2+ players)
- Drinks of choice (keep it responsible!)
- Deck of cards or phone for dares
- Access to a weather app or local forecast
- Optional: Drinking Dojo’s [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) for extra spice

### How to Play

**Step 1: Forecast Roulette**
- Before you start, everyone guesses what the Louisville weather will do in the next hour: rain, sun, wind, or wild temperature swings.
- Wrong guess? Take a drink. Nailed it? Assign a dare to anyone else.

**Step 2: Weather Wildcards**
- Shuffle a deck of cards. Assign each suit to a type of weather:
    - Hearts: Sunny
    - Diamonds: Rainy
    - Clubs: Windy
    - Spades: Cloudy/Stormy  
- Draw a card every 10 minutes. Whatever the suit, act out a dare or challenge based on the weather:
    - **Sunny**: Compliment the person to your left, or take a drink if you can’t think of one.
    - **Rainy**: Waterfall! Everyone drinks until the person before them stops.
    - **Windy**: Spin in a circle and try to walk a straight line. Fail? Drink.
    - **Cloudy/Stormy**: NSFW dare challenge (get creative or use the Drinking Dojo meme pack).

**Step 3: Meme the Weather**
- Every time Louisville’s weather changes (check the app!), the last person to notice has to perform a viral meme dare. Think TikTok dances, dramatic weather forecasts, or your best “it’s raining men” performance.
