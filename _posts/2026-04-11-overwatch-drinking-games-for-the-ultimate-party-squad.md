### Basic Rules

**Take a sip whenever:**
- You get eliminated by an ultimate.
- You miss your ult (and everyone roasts you).
- You accidentally fall off the map.
- Your team wipes (ouch).
- Someone picks Hanzo and says, “Trust me, I’m cracked.”

**Finish your drink if:**
- You get play of the game with a support hero.
- You clutch a round solo.
- You get a team kill (team drinks in your honor).

**Dare Challenges:**
- Each time you lose a round, the losing team draws a dare from a prepared list (or make your own with Drinking Dojo’s custom tools).
- Example dares: Speak in a Reinhardt accent for five minutes, meme spam in chat, or swap to your least played hero for a round.

### House Rules For Extra Mayhem

- **Ultimate Roulette:** Before each match, everyone picks a random hero. Every time you use your ult, you have to do a shot.
- **Role Switch:** If you get called out for bad play, swap roles and drink.
- **Meme Challenge:** The first person to make the group laugh with a meme or voice line hands out two drinks.

## Overwatch Drinking Game Variations

Want to shake it up? Try these:

- **Couples drinking games:** Pair up—when your partner dies, you drink.
- **Wedding party games:** Make Overwatch the highlight of your after-party with custom dares for the newlyweds.
- **House party ideas:** Set up Overwatch stations for tournament-style drinking challenges.

## Safety First

Remember: Know your limits, look out for your friends, and never pressure anyone to drink more than they want. Water and snacks are always a good move, too.
