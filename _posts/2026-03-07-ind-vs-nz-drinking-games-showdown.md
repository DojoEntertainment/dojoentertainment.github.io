## Ind vs NZ Drinking Game Rules

**Set Up:**  
Split your crew into Team India and Team New Zealand—or mix it up if you just want maximum chaos.

### Basic Rules

- **Every 4 or 6:** Everyone takes a sip. If your team’s batter hits it, you nominate someone else.
- **Wicket Falls:** The entire losing team drinks. Add a dare challenge for extra spice!
- **LBW or Controversial Umpire Decision:** All players vote on the call—wrong guess drinks twice.
- **Boundary Save:** Last person to shout “SAVED” drinks.
- **Player Injury Timeout:** Make a toast “to the fallen”—everyone drinks.

### Meme Drinking Game Twists

- **Commentator Dad Joke:** First to laugh does an NSFW dare (or takes a shot).
- **Awkward Player Interview:** Everyone finishes their drink if you cringe.
