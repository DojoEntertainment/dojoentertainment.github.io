## NCAA Wrestling Drinking Game Rules

### Basic Gameplay

1. **Pick Your Wrestler:** Everyone claims a wrestler or team for the match. Whenever your pick scores, you assign a drink. If they get pinned, you drink.
2. **Scoring Sips:**
    - **Takedown:** 1 sip
    - **Escape:** 1 sip
    - **Reversal:** 2 sips
    - **Near Fall:** 2 sips
    - **Pin:** Finish your drink!
3. **Coach Cam:** If the camera cuts to a coach losing their mind, everyone drinks.
4. **Challenge Flag:** When a coach throws a challenge or the ref reviews a call, everyone votes—if majority thinks it’ll get overturned, take a collective sip if you’re wrong.
