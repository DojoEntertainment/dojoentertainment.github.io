## 1. Cinco Sips: A Simple Yet Wild May 5 Drinking Game

The simplest (but surprisingly chaotic) May 5 drinking game. All you need is your drink and a readiness to follow the rules—no props, no prep.

**How to Play:**
- Whenever someone says “cinco,” everyone takes a sip.
- If someone forgets and says “five” instead of “cinco,” they have to do a dare challenge picked by the group.
- Add a meme twist: Anyone who sends a meme in the party group chat gets to pick someone to drink.

*Perfect for mixing in NSFW dares if your group’s up for it—just keep it friendly!*

## 2. The Fiesta Flip Cup: Classic Party Drinking Games for Big Groups

Classic flip cup, May 5 style. This party drinking game is perfect for big groups and makes every round a showdown.

**What You Need:**
- Solo cups (one per player)
- A table
- Your drink of choice

**How to Play:**
1. Split into two teams.
2. Each player must drink, then flip their cup upside down by flicking the rim.
3. First team to finish wins; losing team completes a dare or drinking challenge.
   
**Pro tip:** Use our [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) to create wild dares for the losing side!

## 3. Meme Match: Viral Drinking Games Meet Memes

Bring viral drinking games to the next level with Meme Match—a game where memes and drinks collide.

**What You Need:**
- Phones and a group chat, or a TV with screen sharing
- Drinks

**How to Play:**
1. Pick a theme (May 5 fails, fiesta fashion, awkward party photos—you get it).
2. Each round, everyone sends their best meme into the chat or onto the TV.
3. The group votes on the funniest meme. The winner assigns drinks; the loser does a dare.
   
*Bonus: This doubles as a killer house party idea and keeps everyone on their toes.*

## 4. Dare Roulette: Spice Up Your May 5 with Dare Challenges & NSFW Dares

This one’s for the bold. Add a spicy edge to your May 5 party with dare challenges that range from hilarious to outrageous.

**What You Need:**
- Slips of paper or a dare app
- Drinks

**How to Play:**
1. Write out a mix of drinking challenges and NSFW dares.
2. Players draw a dare at random whenever they land on a designated number (like every fifth drink or every time someone shouts “Margarita!”).
3. Complete the dare or double your drinks.

*Perfect for couples drinking games or big groups—just set your own boundaries!*
