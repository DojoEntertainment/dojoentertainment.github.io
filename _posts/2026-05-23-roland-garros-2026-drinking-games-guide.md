## Roland Garros 2026 Drinking Game Rules

Here’s our signature Roland Garros 2026 party drinking game—easy to set up, impossible to forget.

### Basic Rules

Take a sip, chug, or complete a dare whenever any of these happen:

- **Ace Serve:** The server blasts an ace—everyone cheers, last to raise their glass takes a drink.
- **Double Fault:** Player double-faults—everyone drinks.
- **Rally Over 15 Shots:** The rally keeps going and going—everyone picks a partner; the loser of rock-paper-scissors between each pair drinks.
- **Break Point Drama:** A player breaks serve—group chooses one person to do a dare challenge.
- **Commentator Drops a Meme-Worthy Line:** First to shout “Meme!” assigns a drink.
- **Epic Tantrum or Racket Smash:** Last to mimic the meltdown does a shot.
- **Rain Delay:** All players must invent a new dare. Best one gets to make someone else drink.

### Bonus Dare Challenges

- **Fortnite Drinking Game Crossover:** If someone mentions a “battle” or “drop shot,” the group does a Fortnite dance; worst dancer drinks.
- **Couples Drinking Games Twist:** Watching with your partner? If the camera pans to a tennis couple in the stands, you both take a sip and share a dare.
- **Wedding Party Games Vibe:** For extra spice, every time the crowd does a Mexican wave, the host assigns a wild dare.
