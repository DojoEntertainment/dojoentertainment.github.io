## How to Play: Tour de France 2026 Drinking Game Rules

We’ve kept it simple so you can focus on fun (and maybe a little chaos):

### Take a Sip Every Time:

- The commentators mention a rider’s “strategy”
- There’s a crash or near-miss (stay safe, cyclists!)
- A rider grabs a water bottle or snacks from the team car
- Anyone mentions “yellow jersey”

### Take a Big Gulp If:

- A rider breaks away from the peloton
- A meme-worthy fan runs alongside the cyclists in costume (you know it will happen)
- The camera zooms in on a celebrity in the crowd

### Dare Challenges:

Whenever a commercial break hits or there’s a lull in the action, draw a dare from your stack or use Drinking Dojo’s [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) to keep things wild. Some ideas:

- **NSFW dares** (if your group is up for it)
- Try to pronounce the wildest French town name on the route
- Do your best Tour de France commentator impression
- Invent a new party meme on the spot

### Last Lap Challenge:

On the final lap or legendary mountain stage, everyone picks a rider to “bet” on. If your rider wins the segment, you hand out drinks. If not, you finish your glass!
