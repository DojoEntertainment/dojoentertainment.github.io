### How to Play

1. **Set Up the Clips:** Queue up a playlist of Gaten Matarazzo moments—fan edits, funny interviews, or iconic Stranger Things episodes.
2. **Deal the Dare Cards:** Each player draws a dare card or uses the custom drinking game builder to generate a challenge.
3. **Take a Sip When…**

4. **Dare Challenge:** If you miss a cue or forget to drink, draw from the NSFW dares pile or take a double sip.
5. **Finish Your Drink If…**
