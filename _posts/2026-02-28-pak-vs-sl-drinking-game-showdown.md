## How to Play the Pak vs SL Drinking Game

This party drinking game blends cricket action with drinking challenges and meme-worthy dares. Here’s how to kick things off:

### 1. Squad Up and Pick Sides

- Divide everyone into Team Pak or Team SL.
- If you’ve got an odd number, let someone be the wild card—switching teams after each wicket!

### 2. Lay Down the Rules

**Take a sip when:**
- Your team scores a boundary (four or six)
- The opposing team takes a wicket
- Commentators mention a player’s nickname

**Finish your drink if:**
- There’s a controversial umpire decision
- Someone drops a sitter (an easy catch)

**Draw a dare card when:**
- A player gets out for a duck (zero runs)
- There’s a hat-trick (three wickets in a row)
- The match gets interrupted by rain

### 3. Dare Challenges and Meme Moments

Spice things up with meme drinking games and dare challenges from Drinking Dojo or craft your own. Think:
- NSFW dares for the brave
- Meme-inspired tasks (“Do your best commentator impression”)
- Physical challenges (“Run a lap around the living room for every wide ball”)

### 4. Bonus Party Rules

- **Wedding Party Games Twist:** If the match is at a wedding afterparty, the losing team must perform a group dance.
- **Couples Drinking Games:** Couples on opposing teams? Loser does a viral TikTok dance.
- **House Party Ideas:** Award extra points for best-dressed cricket fan.
