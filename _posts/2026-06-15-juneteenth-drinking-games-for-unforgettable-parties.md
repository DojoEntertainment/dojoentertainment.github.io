## Juneteenth Drinking Game Ideas

We’ve rounded up some of the best drinking games and custom dare challenges for every kind of party—plus a few themed twists to keep things spicy.

### 1. Freedom Toast

**What You Need:**  
- Drinks of choice (keep it classic or mix up Juneteenth-inspired cocktails)  
- Shot glasses or cups  
- A playlist of Black artists’ anthems  

**How to Play:**  
1. Everyone grabs a drink and sits in a circle.  
2. One person makes a toast to freedom, history, or anything that needs celebrating.  
3. After each toast, everyone drinks.  
4. The next person adds their toast, but it can’t repeat what was already said. Get creative—think heroes, milestones, or just the best BBQ sides.  
5. If someone repeats a toast or gets stuck, they take a dare challenge (choose from the Drinking Dojo NSFW dares or create your own).  

**Why It Rocks:**  
It’s easy, inclusive, and a perfect icebreaker—plus, you can slide in some viral drinking games energy by encouraging the wildest, funniest, or most heartfelt toasts.

### 2. Juneteenth Meme Drinking Game

**What You Need:**  
- A phone or tablet  
- Group chat or screen for sharing memes  
- Drinks  

**How to Play:**  
1. Everyone takes turns sending a Juneteenth-related meme or celebration meme to the group.  
2. Each person rates the meme from 1 (meh) to 5 (legendary).  
3. The sender drinks for every 1 or 2 their meme gets. Everyone else drinks for every 4 or 5.  
4. The lowest-rated meme of the round does a dare challenge—try one from our custom drinking game builder for a party twist.  

**Why It Rocks:**  
No more scrolling in silence—this one’s made for meme lovers and works for both in-person and virtual parties.

### 3. House Party Juneteenth Trivia

**What You Need:**  
- Printed or digital Juneteenth trivia questions (make your own or find them online)  
- Drinks  
- A host  

**How to Play:**  
1. The host asks a Juneteenth trivia question to each player.  
2. Get it right, nominate someone to drink. Get it wrong, you drink and pick up a dare challenge.  
3. Bonus: Add NSFW dares or wedding party games for even more chaos.  

**Why It Rocks:**  
It’s a drinking game with a side of knowledge—perfect for sparking convo and a little friendly competition.
