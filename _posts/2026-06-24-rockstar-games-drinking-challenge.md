## How To Play Rockstar Games

### Setup

1. Shuffle the challenge cards and place them in the center.
2. Nominate a Rockstar Judge — this person rotates after every round.
3. Everyone grabs a drink and gets ready for mayhem.

### Game Flow

1. **Draw a Card:** The Rockstar Judge picks a card and reads it aloud.
2. **Challenge or Dare:** The card will feature either:
    - A *meme drinking game* (e.g., “Take a sip every time someone quotes a TikTok sound”)
    - A *wild dare* (e.g., “Swap shirts with the person on your left or finish your drink”)
    - A *group challenge* (e.g., “Everyone who’s played Fortnite does a shoey — last to finish drinks twice”)
    - An *NSFW dare* (e.g., “Send a risky meme to your ex or take two shots”)
3. **Complete or Drink:** The player (or group) must complete the challenge. If they refuse, they drink the penalty stated on the card.
4. **Pass the Judge:** The Rockstar Judge spot rotates to the left for the next round.

### Winning

Let’s be honest — in Rockstar Games, everyone wins. But for a competitive twist, the Rockstar Judge can award a “Legend” point each round to the player with the boldest moves. First to 5 points gets crowned the Rockstar of the night — and the rest finish their drinks in their honor.
