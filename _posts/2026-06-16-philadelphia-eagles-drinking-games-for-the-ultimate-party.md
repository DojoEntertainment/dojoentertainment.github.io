### Basic Rules

1. **Every time the Eagles score a touchdown:** Take two sips.
2. **If the Eagles’ defense gets a turnover:** Everyone cheers and takes a shot.
3. **When commentators mention “underdog” or “Philly Special”:** Last person to touch their nose takes a dare.
4. **If the camera shows a fan in Eagles gear:** Everyone yells “Go Birds!” and takes a sip.
5. **If there’s a meme-able moment (think epic celebrations or wild fan signs):** Assign a meme drinking game dare to someone in the group.
