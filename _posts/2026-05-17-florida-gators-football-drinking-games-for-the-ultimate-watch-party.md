## Game 1: Gator Chomp Challenge

This one’s for the die-hard fans who live for every touchdown and wild play.

**How to Play:**
- Every time the Gators score a touchdown, everyone does the iconic Gator Chomp — losers (the slowest chompers) take two sips.
- If the opposing team fumbles, everyone yells “Go Gators!” — last one yells takes a dare challenge.
- Whenever a Gators meme or viral moment pops up on social media, everyone takes a drink.
- If the commentators mention “Swamp” or talk about the heat, everyone has to hydrate (water counts!).

*Bonus*: Add NSFW dares for those feeling brave. Just pull a card from your dare deck whenever a challenge is missed.

## Game 2: Meme Drinking Game – Florida Gators Edition

Nothing brings people together like laughing at meme-worthy plays and sideline moments.

**How to Play:**
- Before the game, everyone submits their favorite Gators memes or classic viral moments.
- Each time a similar play or meme-worthy event happens, the person who picked that meme assigns drinks or dares.
- If someone gets caught checking their phone for scores from other college games, they do a dare or take a big sip.
