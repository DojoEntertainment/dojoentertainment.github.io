## What You Need for The Dark Wizard Drinking Game

- **A group of friends** (4+ works best)
- **A deck of standard playing cards**
- **Drinks of choice** (beer, cocktails, mocktails, you do you)
- **A table or circle to sit around**
- **Optional:** A wizard hat or funky prop for extra drama

## Setting Up The Dark Wizard Game

1. **Shuffle the deck** and place it face-down in the center.
2. Appoint the first Dark Wizard (whoever most recently lost a dare challenge or, for laughs, whoever can do the best evil laugh).
3. The Dark Wizard puts on the wizard hat (if you’ve got one) and takes the “Wizard Throne” — a spot everyone can see.

## How To Play The Dark Wizard Drinking Game

1. **Draw and Obey:**  
   Each player, starting left of the Dark Wizard, draws a card. Every card has a unique rule (see below).

2. **Wizard’s Rule:**  
   The Dark Wizard can invent a new rule at any time. Breaking the rule means you drink!

3. **Dare Challenges:**  
   If you draw a face card, the Dark Wizard gets to assign you a dare — make it wild or meme-worthy. Think viral drinking games meet meme drinking games.

4. **Passing the Hat:**  
   If someone draws an Ace, they become the new Dark Wizard and take the hat. Time for a power shift and fresh chaos.

5. **Game End:**  
   Play until the deck runs out or everyone’s crying from laughter.

### Card Rules Cheat Sheet for Your Party Drinking Games

- **2–6:**  
  Take that number of sips.

- **7:**  
  Heaven — everyone raises a hand, last to do it drinks.

- **8:**  
  Mate — pick someone to drink with you.

- **9:**  
  Rhyme Time — say a word, next person rhymes, whoever fails drinks.

- **10:**  
  Categories — name a category (like “Fortnite skins” or “types of beer”), go around until someone blanks.

- **Jack/Queen/King:**  
  The Dark Wizard gives you a dare challenge. NSFW dares for the brave, or keep it chill with meme dares.

- **Ace:**  
  The Dark Wizard’s powers transfer to you!
