## What Is the Road To UFC Drinking Game?

Think: the intensity of the Octagon, but with drinks, dares, and a whole lot of laughter. This party drinking game is all about friendly (or not-so-friendly) competition, wild dare challenges, and the bragging rights that come with being the last one standing. Customize the rules, throw in some NSFW dares if your crew is up for it, and you’ve got a new legend in the making—perfect for UFC fans, meme drinking games lovers, or anyone who’s down for epic drinking challenges.

- **Drinks** (beer, seltzers, cocktails—your call)
- **Shot glasses or cups**
- **Printed or digital Road To UFC Drinking Game rules**
- **A timer or phone stopwatch**
- **Notepad (digital or paper) for scorekeeping**
- **Optional: Custom dare cards, costumes, or meme props**

## How To Play the Road To UFC Drinking Game

It’s simple. Choose your fighters, set the rounds, and get ready to throw down. Here’s the breakdown:

### 1. Set Up the “Octagon”

- Clear a space in your living room, backyard, dorm, or any house party idea location. The playing area is your “octagon.”
- Split players into two teams or battle it out one-on-one, tournament-style.

### 2. Choose Your Rounds

- Decide how many rounds to play (3, 5, or go wild with a championship 7).
- Each round features a different dare challenge or drinking game mini-round.

### 3. Pick Your Dare Challenges

Mix it up with these ideas:

- **Punchline Prowess**: Tell your best meme-worthy joke. Loser drinks.
- **Stamina Showdown**: Hold a plank or wall sit. First to drop, drinks.
- **Trivia Takedown**: UFC or meme trivia. Wrong answer? Take a shot.
- **Fortnite Drinking Game Round**: Quick game of Fortnite-inspired drinking challenges for bonus points.
- **NSFW Dares**: Feeling bold? Draw from a stack of NSFW dares for the ultimate test of courage.

### 4. Scoring and Sipping

- Winner of each round assigns drinks to the loser.
- Keep track of points—first to 3 (or 5) wins claims the title (and ultimate bragging rights).

### 5. Bonus Rounds

Up the ante with:

- **Sudden Death**: Tie-breaker round, winner takes all.
- **Custom Drinking Game Builder**: Use Drinking Dojo’s [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) to add your own wild rules or dares.

### 6. Celebrate Like a Champ

- The winner gets a prize (like a championship belt, meme trophy, or just a killer selfie moment).
- Loser does a final dare, chosen by the winner.
