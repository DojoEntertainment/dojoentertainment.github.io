## When Is the Coca Cola 600 Start Time?

Before you break out the drinks, make sure you and your mates have the start time locked in. The Coca Cola 600 usually drops on the Sunday of Memorial Day Weekend (late May), with the green flag waving in the evening (around 6:00 PM ET in the US). Double-check local listings if you’re tuning in from the UK, Canada, Australia, or Denmark—time zones matter when drinks are on the line!

## Epic Coca Cola 600 Drinking Game Rules

All you need is a love for viral drinking games, a few cold ones, and your rowdiest friends. Here’s how to set up your ultimate Coca Cola 600 party drinking game:

### What You’ll Need

- Drinks of choice (beer, cider, cocktails, or soft drinks if you’re keeping it chill)
- Printed or digital list of rules
- A TV or streaming device
- Optional: themed props (race flags, hats, or meme drinking games cards)

### Basic Rules

**Take a Sip:**
- Every time a car overtakes another.
- Whenever the commentators mention “endurance.”
- If someone in the room shouts at the TV.

**Take Two Sips:**
- Every pit stop.
- When there’s a replay of a crash.
- When the camera shows a celebrity in the crowd.

**Finish Your Drink:**
- If your chosen driver wins a stage.
- When there’s an unexpected red flag.
- If a meme-worthy moment happens on Twitter during the race.

**Dare Challenges:**
- If your favorite driver crashes out, you must complete a dare challenge chosen by the group (think NSFW dares if you’re feeling bold, or keep it silly and safe).
- Last person to stand up during a commercial break has to take a dare.

### Couples Drinking Games Twist

Pair up and pick a driver for each duo. If your driver falls behind, your team drinks. If your driver pulls ahead, assign a dare to another couple. It’s a fun way to turn the Coca Cola 600 start time into a romantic (and hilarious) showdown.
