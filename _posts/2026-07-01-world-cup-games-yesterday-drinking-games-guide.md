## Turn Every Match Into a Drinking Game Party

The beauty of party drinking games is they make every goal, foul, and dramatic flop way more exciting. Here’s how to get started with your own world cup-inspired drinking session:

### What You’ll Need

- TVs or streaming devices to watch the match highlights or replays (YouTube works!)
- A variety of drinks (beer, cocktails, seltzers—your call)
- Cups or shot glasses for each player
- Access to [Drinking Dojo’s custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) for easy rule creation
- Optional: Meme cards, a whiteboard for scorekeeping, and props for NSFW dares

## Top Drinking Games for World Cup Vibes

### 1. **Goal Gulp**

**How to Play:**
- Every time a team scores a goal, everyone supporting the other team takes a drink.
- If a player celebrates with a wild dance, the person who last texted in the group chat takes two sips.
- Own goal? Everyone drinks.

**Pro Tip:** Add dare challenges for each goal (like singing the scoring team’s anthem).

### 2. **Yellow Card, Red Card**

**How to Play:**
- Yellow card: The player most likely to break the rules (be honest, you know who you are) takes a shot.
- Red card: Everyone does a group dare. Use the custom drinking game builder to spin up something wild, or grab an NSFW dare from Drinking Dojo’s packs.
- If the ref checks VAR, last person to shout “VAR!” drinks.

### 3. **Meme Reaction Madness**

**How to Play:**
- Pause the game after every dramatic flop, missed penalty, or hilarious fan reaction.
- The group votes for the best meme caption—loser drinks.
- Bonus: Share your memes in your group chat for viral drinking games vibes.

### 4. **Commentator Cliché Challenge**

**How to Play:**
- Every time the commentator says classic phrases like “It’s coming home,” “Back of the net,” or “Unbelievable scenes,” everyone takes a sip.
- For every British or Aussie slang word, the group nominates someone to do a dare challenge.

## House Party Ideas with a World Cup Twist

- **Fortnite Drinking Game Crossover:** When a player celebrates like a Fortnite emote, everyone does the move (loser drinks).
- **Couples Drinking Games:** Pair up—whenever there’s a close-up on couples or famous duos in the crowd, both drink!
- **Wedding Party Games:** If you’re watching with a wedding crowd, make the couple drink for every goal (or turn it into a dance-off).
