## What Is the Eagles Drinking Game?

Eagles is a viral drinking game built for groups who love a bit of chaos and don’t mind being put on the spot. Play it with just a deck of cards and your favorite drinks — no complicated setup required.

## How to Play Eagles

1. **Everyone Sits in a Circle**  
   Pour your drinks, get comfy, and make sure everyone can see the deck in the center.

2. **Deal the Cards**  
   Dealer flips cards one at a time into the center of the table, face up.

3. **Eagle Call**  
   The moment a Queen is revealed, anyone can shout “EAGLE!” as fast as possible.  
   - The first person to shout “EAGLE!” points to another player and gives them a dare or challenge.  
   - The chosen player can either **complete the dare** (think house party ideas like singing, dancing, or answering a spicy question) or **take a drink**.

4. **Extra Dare Challenges**  
   Want to spice it up? Toss in viral drinking game dares or meme drinking games rules. Every time two Queens show up back-to-back, everyone must finish their drink or face a group dare.

5. **Winning the Game**  
   There’s no official winner in Eagles — you play until the deck runs out, the drinks do, or everyone’s out of wild party energy.

## House Rules and Variations

- **NSFW Dares:** For adult groups, up the ante with cheeky dares that get everyone blushing.  
- **Fortnite Drinking Game Crossover:** When a Queen is drawn, last one to shout “EAGLE!” must do a Fortnite dance or drink.  
- **Wedding Party Games:** Switch to “LOVE BIRD” instead of “EAGLE” for wedding receptions — perfect for bringing the families together (and breaking the ice).  
- **Election Day or Inauguration Day Themes:** Swap the eagle for a political meme — just don’t get too heated!
