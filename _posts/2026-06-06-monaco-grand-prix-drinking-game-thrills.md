### Basic Rules

1. **Pre-Race Toast**  
   Everyone raises a glass and picks their driver. If your driver wins, you make a dare for someone else. If they crash out, you take a dare challenge yourself.

2. **Drink When…**  
   - A car overtakes on the track  
   - The commentators mention "tight corners" or "glamour"  
   - Someone crashes (everybody drinks)  
   - There’s a pit stop longer than 5 seconds  
   - The camera shows a yacht or celebrity  
   - The crowd goes wild

3. **Wild Card Dares**  
   Use NSFW dares or custom challenges—lose a bet, do a dare from the Drinking Dojo pack, or pull a meme drinking game card.

4. **Final Lap Frenzy**  
   As the race enters the last 5 laps, everyone takes a sip every time the leader changes, or if a commentator says “unbelievable.”

5. **Victory Lap**  
   When the winner crosses the finish line, losers must finish their drinks or pull a dare challenge. Winner gets meme king or queen status for the night.
