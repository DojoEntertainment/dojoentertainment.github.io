## How to Play the Jannik Sinner Drinking Game: Party Drinking Games Rules

This party drinking game is as easy as a smooth serve. Just follow the rules below and let the good times roll — or, you know, volley.

### Basic Rules for Drinking Games

Every time one of these Jannik Sinner moments happens during a match, everyone takes a sip (or a shot, if you’re feeling brave):

- **Jannik Sinner wins a set**: Everybody drinks
- **He hits an ace**: Last person to shout “Ace!” drinks
- **He challenges a call**: Make a dare (NSFW dares welcome if you’re up for it)
- **He wins a long rally (10 shots or more)**: Make a toast to Sinner’s stamina and drink
- **Camera catches Sinner smiling**: Group selfie time, and everyone drinks
- **He misses an easy shot**: The group assigns a dare to the player who predicted he’d win the point

### Bonus Dare Challenges for Viral Drinking Games

Want to kick it up a notch and make things even more viral? Try these meme drinking games twists:

- **Fortnite Victory Royale**: If Sinner does a fist pump, the group does a “Fortnite dance” (or your best attempt) — loser drinks
- **Election Day Shoutout**: If the commentators mention any other player’s political views, everyone drinks twice
- **Couples Drinking Games**: Pair up and every time Sinner wins a break point, couples must swap drinks
