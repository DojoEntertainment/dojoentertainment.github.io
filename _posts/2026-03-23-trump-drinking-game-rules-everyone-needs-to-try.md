## What Is the Trump Drinking Game?

The trump drinking game isn’t about politics — it’s all about cards, laughs, and epic dares. It’s a mashup of traditional card game mechanics and the wild energy of meme drinking games, perfect for groups who want their drinking challenges to come with a side of chaos.

## How To Play the Trump Drinking Game

### Setup
1. Gather everyone in a circle.
2. Shuffle the deck and deal all cards evenly.
3. Decide on your favorite drink and keep it handy.

### Gameplay

**The Basics:**  
- Each round, players play one card face-up. The highest card (the trump card) wins the round.
- Suit order: Spades > Hearts > Diamonds > Clubs.
- If two players tie, it’s time for a dare challenge!

**Round Structure:**  
1. Everyone picks a card from their hand and lays it down at the same time.
2. The player with the highest card in the trump suit wins.  
   - If no trump suit was announced, the highest card of any suit wins.
3. The round loser has to take a drink.
4. For an extra kick, the round winner can assign a dare from the meme drinking games list (think: “do your best Fortnite dance” or “send a wild DM”).
5. If there’s a tie, both players must complete an NSFW dare or double shot (your group decides how spicy you want it).

### Making It Wild

- **Custom Dare Challenges:** Use Drinking Dojo’s [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) to add viral dares, wedding party games, or even election day drinking game rules to your trump night.
- **Special Cards:** Assign wild house rules for jokers (e.g., everyone drinks, or the joker draws a dare from the meme pack).
- **Couples Drinking Games Twist:** Partners play as a team, and if they lose a round, both drink or complete a dare together.

### Winning

- Play until all cards are gone, or your drink supply runs dry.
- The player (or team) with the fewest drinks taken at the end earns the title of “Trump Master” — and ultimate bragging rights.
