## Chiefs Touchdown Toast

A classic, easy game perfect for any group.

**Rules:**
- Every time the Chiefs score a touchdown, everyone raises their drink and takes a big sip.
- If Travis Kelce scores, take an extra sip.
- If the Chiefs go for a two-point conversion, everyone has to finish their drink.

**Dare Challenge:**  
When Patrick Mahomes throws a no-look pass, the last person to shout “Mahomie!” does a dare from the group.

## Meme Drinking Game Moments

Let’s get a little wild with some meme drinking games—because what’s a Chiefs party without viral moments and TikTok-worthy dares?

**Rules:**
- Take a sip every time a Taylor Swift song gets mentioned during the broadcast.
- When the camera shows Andy Reid’s mustache, everyone has to share their best meme face—best one gives out three sips.
- Any time commentators bring up the Chiefs’ past Super Bowl wins, everyone does a quick “cheers” and drinks.

**NSFW Option:**  
If you’re hosting a late-night Chiefs bash, add an NSFW dare for every missed field goal. (Keep it friendly and fun!)

## Chiefs Drinking Game Bingo

Time to put your party drinking games skills to the test.

**How to Play:**
- Make a simple bingo card with classic Chiefs game moments (interceptions, Kelce dance, Mahomes headband, ref throws a flag, etc.).
- When something on your card happens, take a sip.
- First to bingo gets to invent a dare challenge for the whole group.

## Couples Drinking Games
