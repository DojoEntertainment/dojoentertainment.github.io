## How to Play the Wordle Drinking Game — Step-by-Step

Here’s how to turn the world’s favorite word puzzle into one of your new favorite drinking challenges:

### 1. Set Up the Game

- Gather your group and fire up Wordle — the New York Times on a shared screen.
- Decide if you’ll play as one team or compete individually. Team play is great for larger groups and wedding party games.

### 2. Establish the Drinking Rules

Add a shot of mayhem with these simple, meme-worthy rules:

- **Wrong Letter, Take a Sip:** Every time a guess contains a letter not in the answer, take a sip.
- **Yellow Letter, Dare Time:** For every yellow (correct letter, wrong spot), draw a dare card or use our custom dare challenges. Think NSFW dares for spicy nights or keep it chill for casual hangs.
- **Gray Out, Pass the Drink:** If your guess is all gray, pass your drink to someone else and they take a big gulp.
- **Green Letter, Give a Sip:** For every green (correct letter, right spot), choose someone to take a sip.
- **Failed Wordle, Finish Your Drink:** Didn’t solve it in six tries? Chug the rest of your drink or complete a viral drinking game challenge from our site.

### 3. Spice Up the Game with Custom Challenges

- Swap in special rounds for inauguration day drinking game or election day drinking game vibes during big events.
