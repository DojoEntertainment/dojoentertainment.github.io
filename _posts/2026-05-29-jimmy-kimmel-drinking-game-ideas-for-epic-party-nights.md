## Jimmy Kimmel Drinking Game Rules

Here’s how to turn any episode into the ultimate viral drinking game:

### Basic Rules

Take a sip* whenever:
- Jimmy makes a joke about a celebrity’s name.
- There’s a viral meme or internet challenge featured.
- A guest mentions another late-night host.
- Guillermo pops up on screen.
- Someone plugs their latest movie or project.

Take a shot if:
- Jimmy plays a prank or dares a guest.
- There’s an awkward or NSFW moment.
- The band plays someone on or off the stage.

Finish your drink if:
- A celebrity drops a major spoiler or secret.
- The crowd goes wild for an unexpected guest.

\*Adjust sips, shots, or dares to your tolerance and local drinking laws. Always play responsibly!

## Tips For The Ultimate Jimmy Kimmel Party

- Mix in meme drinking games for extra laughs — try pausing after a viral moment and letting the group create a meme on the spot.
- For NSFW dares, make sure everyone’s on board and comfortable.
- Add a regional twist: Canadians, cheers when someone mentions hockey. Brits, sip when a guest has an accent. Aussies, drink when there’s a kangaroo joke (trust us, it happens).
- Looking for more inspiration? Try pairing with other themed games like the Fortnite drinking game or even a pop culture-packed inauguration day drinking game.
