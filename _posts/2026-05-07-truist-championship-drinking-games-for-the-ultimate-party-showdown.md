## Truist Championship Drinking Game Rules

### **1. Pre-Game Draft**

Before the Truist Championship kicks off, everyone picks a team or player to cheer for. If your pick wins a round, everyone else takes a drink. If your pick fails epically, you drink twice.

### **2. Play-by-Play Sips**

- **Scoreboard Switch:** Every time the lead changes, everyone drinks.
- **Epic Move:** Someone pulls off a wild play or meme-worthy moment? Last person to react does a dare.
- **Timeout Tactics:** During every official timeout or ad break, the group must choose a dare challenge for one unlucky player.

### **3. Dare Challenges**

Spice it up with viral drinking games and NSFW dares (if your crowd’s up for it):

- **Dare Roulette:** Use the Drinking Dojo custom drinking game builder to generate dares. If you refuse your dare, finish your drink.
- **Meme Madness:** Share the best meme from the championship so far. The worst meme, as voted by the group, does a shot.

### **4. Ultimate Showdown**

At the end of the championship, the person whose team or player came in last place must take on the “Championship Challenge”—a wild group dare (think: dramatic karaoke, TikTok dance, or a wedding party games-style toast).
