## London Marathon 2027 Drinking Game Rules

### The Basics

**Players:** 2 to infinity  
**Duration:** As long as the marathon (or until you tap out)  

### How To Play

1. **Pick Your Runner:**  
   Everyone chooses a runner (celebrity, costume legend, or random pick). Every time your runner is mentioned on screen, you take a sip.

2. **Meme Moments:**  
   Spot a wild costume, a runner in a banana suit, or someone dressed as Big Ben? Everyone shouts “Marathon Mayhem!” and does a group shot.

3. **Dare Challenges:**  
   When a commentator drops a classic British phrase (“Keep calm and carry on!”), the last person to shout “Cheers!” draws an NSFW dare or wild challenge from the pile.

4. **Finish Line Frenzy:**  
   When the first runner crosses the finish line, everyone does a victory dance. The person whose runner finishes last must do the ultimate dare challenge.

5. **Hydration Station:**  
   Every time the camera cuts to someone drinking water, everyone has to hydrate (sip your drink — or maybe actual water if you need to pace yourself).

6. **Unexpected Events:**  
   - **Weather chaos** (rain, wind, sun): Everyone drinks.  
   - **Celebrity sighting:** First person to spot and shout the celeb's name gives out two drinks.

### Bonus Rounds

- **Couples Drinking Games Twist:**  
   Pair up. If your runners finish back-to-back, swap drinks for the next round.

- **Wedding Party Games Vibe:**  
   Pass out mini medals for crazy dares completed — the person with the most medals by the end wins eternal party glory.
