## Classic FIFA World Cup Drinking Game Rules and Drinking Challenges

### How to Play

1. **Pick Your Teams:** Everyone chooses a team to support. If your team isn’t playing, pick one to root for.
2. **Set Up the Rules:** Use the list below or create custom ones with Drinking Dojo’s custom drinking game builder.
3. **Start the Game:** Drinks in hand, let the match (and the madness) begin!

### Basic Rules

Take a sip when:
- The ref blows the whistle
- A commentator says “unbelievable”
- Someone flops dramatically

Take two sips if:
- Your team concedes a goal
- There’s a yellow card
- The camera cuts to a wild fan in costume

Finish your drink (or do a shot) if:
- There’s a red card
- A penalty is awarded
- Your team scores a last-minute winner
