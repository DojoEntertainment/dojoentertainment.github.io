## 1. Mountaineer Flip Cup: Classic Party Drinking Game at WVU

Nothing says “college party” like flip cup, but at WVU, it comes with a twist. This meme drinking game is all about speed, coordination, and just a little bit of luck.

**How to Play:**
- Split into two teams, standing on opposite sides of a long table.
- Each player has a cup filled with their drink of choice.
- At “Go,” the first player in line drinks, then tries to flip the cup upside down by flicking the rim.
- Once the cup lands upside down, the next player goes.
- First team to finish wins, losers face dare challenges picked by the winners (think NSFW dares if you’re feeling bold).

**Materials Needed:**  
- Plastic cups  
- Table  
- Drinks (beer, seltzer, or whatever you fancy)  

## 2. WVU Meme Challenge: Viral Drinking Games with Campus Flair

Bring those campus memes to life with a game that blends drinking challenges and viral drinking games energy.

**How to Play:**
- Everyone writes down a classic WVU meme or inside joke (think couch burning, the PRT, or that one wild tailgate).
- Toss them in a hat.
- Players draw and act out the meme. If the group can’t guess it, the performer drinks.

**Materials Needed:**  
- Paper and pens  
- Drinks  
- A hat or bowl  

## 3. Mountaineer Dare Roulette: Exciting NSFW Dare Challenges

You want wild? This is where things get real. Dare roulette takes party drinking games up a notch, with every spin guaranteed to spice up your night.

**How to Play:**
- Write out a bunch of dares (bonus points for NSFW dares or campus-themed challenges).
- Number each dare and write matching numbers on slips of paper.
- Players draw a number and must do the dare—or take two drinks as a penalty.

**Materials Needed:**  
- Paper and pens  
- A bowl  
- Drinks
