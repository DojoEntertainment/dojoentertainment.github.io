#### 1. Set Up the Teams
Split your group into smaller teams. Each team picks (or is randomly assigned) an MLB team currently in the standings. If you want to spice things up, use the [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) to randomize team assignments or add extra dares!

#### 2. Game Rules

- **Start With a Toast**: Everyone drinks once for the team currently in first place.
- **Scoreboard Sips**:
    - If your chosen team is in the top 3: Take 1 drink.
    - If your team is in the bottom 3: Take 3 drinks.
    - If your team is right in the middle: Hand out 2 drinks to anyone.
- **Home Run Dare**: Whenever a team hits a home run during a live game, the corresponding team assigns an NSFW dare to another player.
- **Wild Card Round**:
    - If your team moves up in the standings during the game, you give out a dare challenge.
    - If your team drops, you have to do a meme-worthy impression of your least favorite player or chug your drink.
- **Tie Breaker**: If two teams are tied in the standings, both teams must create a new dare (the group votes on the best one for next round).

#### 3. Add-On Challenges

- Use viral drinking games like “Never Have I Ever” or “Flip Cup” between innings or as penalty rounds.
- Incorporate wedding party games or couples drinking games if you’re celebrating a special occasion.
