## Must-Try Cinco de Mayo Drinking Games

Ready to swap boring beer pong for wild dares and meme-worthy moments? Check out these party drinking games you can play with just a few essentials — and a crew that’s down for anything.

### 1. Margarita Mayhem

**What you’ll need:**
- Margaritas (or your fave tequila drink)
- Deck of cards

**How to play:**
1. Sit in a circle and place the deck in the center.
2. Each player draws a card on their turn.
3. Each card means:
   - **Ace:** Make a rule everyone must follow for the rest of the game (bend those rules, dojo style).
   - **2–6:** Assign that number of sips to anyone.
   - **7:** Dare challenge — the drawer must complete a dare from the group or take a big gulp.
   - **8–10:** Meme round — tell a meme-worthy joke or story, or drink.
   - **Face cards:** Everyone drinks!
4. Break the rules? That’s another round for you.

**Why it rocks:** The custom rules and dares keep things unpredictable, perfect for wild house party ideas.

### 2. Lotería Shots

**What you’ll need:**
- Lotería (Mexican bingo) cards — find printable versions online
- Shot glasses
- Your drink of choice

**How to play:**
1. Hand out Lotería cards to everyone.
2. The caller pulls cards from the deck and calls them out.
3. If you have the card, mark it and assign a drink to another player.
4. First person with a full row yells “Cinco de Drinko” and makes everyone else finish their drink.

**Why it rocks:** A classic with a twist, this viral drinking game brings a bit of friendly competition and a lot of shots.
