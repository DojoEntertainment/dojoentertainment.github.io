## Essential Drinking Games for April 20

### 1. **Meme Mayhem**

**Perfect for:** Groups who love TikToks, memes, and roasting each other  
**Materials:** Meme cards (print your own or use your phones), drinks

**How to Play:**
- Everyone submits their favorite meme (DMs count!) to the group chat.
- Shuffle and pick one at random. The group decides who the meme fits best.
- That person takes a drink and completes a dare challenge, like giving their best impression or telling a story that fits the meme.
- Bonus: Up the stakes with NSFW dares if your group’s game.

**Why it rocks:** It’s a viral drinking game that brings inside jokes and social media culture straight to your party.

### 2. **Custom Dare Challenge Builder**

**Perfect for:** Anyone who wants to spice things up with personalized dares  
**Materials:** [Drinking Dojo’s Custom Drinking Game Builder](https://drinkingdojo.com), drinks, your imagination

**How to Play:**
- Use the custom builder to create a set of dares and challenges tailored to your group.
- Mix in April 20 themes, inside jokes, and trending topics.
- Take turns picking dares. Complete the challenge or drink.

**Why it rocks:** No two games are ever the same, and you can dial the chaos up or down depending on your crowd.

### 3. **Fortnite Drinking Game**

**Perfect for:** Gamers and competitive spirits  
**Materials:** Fortnite (or your fave battle royale game), drinks

**How to Play:**
- Every elimination: take a sip.
- Top 10 finish: everyone else drinks.
- Victory Royale: everyone but the winner finishes their drink.
- First out? You’ve got to complete a dare challenge before getting back in.

**Why it rocks:** It’s an epic mashup of gaming and drinking challenges that keeps everyone engaged, even if you’re just spectating.

### 4. **Couples Meme Drinking Game**

**Perfect for:** Couples or friends who love to stir the pot  
**Materials:** Meme cards, drinks

**How to Play:**
- Each couple picks a meme that sums up their relationship.
- Everyone votes on the funniest, most relatable, or most savage meme.
- Losing couple does a dare or takes a shot.

**Why it rocks:** It’s a hilarious way to see who’s got the best sense of humor (and who’s willing to risk a little embarrassment).

## Pro Tips for an Epic April 20 House Party

- **Mix in dare challenges:** Keep things wild and unpredictable.  
- **Set up a meme station:** Let guests add their own memes and dares to the mix.  
- **Stay stocked:** Don’t run out of drinks or snacks—seriously, no one wants that.  
- **Keep it safe:** Hydrate, pace yourself, and look out for your mates.
