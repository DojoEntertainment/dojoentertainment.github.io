Ready to turn the Cap City Half Marathon into an epic party drinking game? Whether you’re spectating from your living room, cheering curbside, or just looking for a new way to get wild with friends, this is how you take race day to the next level. Lace up your running shoes—or your best party slippers—and get ready for the ultimate drinking game packed with dare challenges, memes, and non-stop laughs.

## What You Need

- Your favorite drinks (beer, seltzer, cocktails, or mocktails)  
- Cap City Half Marathon live stream or TV coverage  
- A group of friends (in person or virtually)  
- Dare cards or a custom drinking game builder (check out [Drinking Dojo’s custom tools](https://drinkingdojo.com) for next-level fun)  
- Optional: Props like race bibs, sweatbands, or medals for extra vibes  

## How To Play

### 1. Set the Stage  
Pick your viewing party spot—couch, backyard, or even a bar. Everyone grabs a drink and splits into teams or plays solo.

### 2. Choose Your Drinking Game Rules  
Mix and match these classic and viral drinking game rules, or create your own using the custom drinking game builder for a personalized experience.

#### Race Day Sip Rules  
Take a sip when:  
- The announcer says "pace" or "hydration"  
- You spot a runner in a wild costume (banana suit, anyone?)  
- Someone waves at the camera  
- A runner stumbles and recovers like a champ  
- The crowd does the wave  

#### Dare Challenges  
Want to spice it up? Pull a dare card every time:  
- The lead runner changes  
- The camera cuts to the finish line  
- A runner high-fives a spectator  

Sample dares:  
- Post your best meme about the race in your group chat  
- Do your most dramatic finish line pose  
- Reenact a runner’s stride in the middle of the room  

#### Meme Drinking Game Bonus  
If you spot a viral moment (someone dancing, a wild sign, or a dog in costume), everyone has to create a meme or come up with a punny caption. The group votes on the best one—the winner gives out three drinks.

#### NSFW and Couples Options  
- For the afterparty, add NSFW dares (think racy charades or truth-or-shots).  
- Couples can turn it into a flirty challenge by creating secret signals and drinking whenever their partner spots a “race crush” on-screen.  

#### Make It Last  
- The last person to finish their drink does a runner’s lunge.  
- The first to spot someone in a tutu gets to assign a dare.
