### General Rules to Get the Party Started

1. **Choose Your Game Mode:** Pick a multiplayer title everyone can join. Fortnite and party brawlers are solid bets.
2. **Assign Drinks:** Each player picks a signature drink for the night.
3. **Set Ground Rules:** Agree on max drinks per round and any NSFW dares you want to include.

### How to Play Xbox Cloud Gaming Drinking Games

Here’s a classic setup, but feel free to customize:

#### Fortnite Drinking Game Rules

- **Eliminated in the First 5 Minutes:** Take 2 sips.
- **Victory Royale:** Everyone else drinks, winner picks a dare challenge.
- **Down but Not Out:** Get knocked, finish your drink or take a dare.
- **Epic Fail (Fall Damage or Self-Elimination):** Meme drinking game style—everyone roasts you, loser drinks.

#### General Xbox Cloud Gaming Party Drinking Games

- **Achievement Unlocked:** First to earn an in-game achievement gets to assign drinks or dares.
- **Respawn Roulette:** Every time you respawn, take a sip. Last player standing gives out a custom dare.
- **NSFW Dare Wheel:** Use the Drinking Dojo custom drinking game builder to spin up wild dares for losers.

### Couples and Group Variations

- **Couples Drinking Games:** Play in duos—if your partner goes down in-game, you drink together and share a dare.
- **Wedding Party Games:** Host a bracket tournament—losing team must complete a dare challenge from the meme drinking games pack.
