### Game Rules

Here’s your cheat sheet for the ultimate Jimmy Kimmel party drinking game:

- **Take a Sip If…**
  - Jimmy mentions “Matt Damon.”
  - A guest makes an awkward joke.
  - Someone plugs their latest movie or project.
  - Guillermo appears on screen.

- **Finish Your Drink If…**
  - Jimmy pulls a prank on someone live.
  - There’s a celebrity roast.
  - A viral meme moment happens.
  - The audience goes wild for a surprise guest.

- **Dare Challenges**
  - Recreate a Kimmel meme — best impression wins, loser drinks.
  - Tell your own embarrassing story (if you chicken out, take two sips).
  - Try to keep a straight face during a cringy interview clip. If you laugh, you drink.

- **Viral Twist Cards** (Optional)
  - If someone can correctly predict Jimmy’s next joke, everyone else drinks.
  - “Inauguration Day” or “Election Day” special: If politics come up, everyone does a group cheers and drinks.
