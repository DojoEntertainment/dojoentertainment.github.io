## The Donut Dunk-Off Drinking Game

This game is simple, wild, and can get competitive fast. Perfect for donut lovers and meme lords alike.

### How to Play

1. **Circle Up:** Gather everyone around a table with the donuts and drinks in the center.  
2. **Donut Selection:** Each player picks a donut. If someone grabs the last Boston Kreme, they must immediately do a dare challenge chosen by the group.  
3. **The Dunk:** Player one dunks their donut in their drink and takes a bite. If they finish their bite without making a face, they give out a drink. If they flinch or hesitate, they must take a shot (or a big sip).  
4. **Meme Round:** After each donut dunk, the group comes up with a meme caption for what just happened. Best meme (voted by the group) gives out an extra drink.  
5. **Dare Challenges:** Every time someone finishes a full donut, they pull a dare card. If they refuse, they must finish their drink.  
6. **Keep Going:** Play continues until the donuts or drinks are gone—or everyone is in a sugar coma.  

## Donut Day Dare Roulette

Feeling riskier? Bring out the dare challenges for a round of Donut Day Dare Roulette. This is perfect for anyone who loves viral drinking games with a twist.

### How to Play

1. **Write Down Dares:** Use slips of paper to write NSFW dares, donut-themed challenges, or classic party dares.  
2. **Spin the Donut:** Place a donut in the center and spin it. Whoever it points to draws a dare.  
3. **Complete or Drink:** The chosen player must either complete the dare or take a big sip.  
4. **Couples Bonus:** If playing as couples, both partners must complete the dare together or both drink.  
5. **Wedding Party Vibes:** Add this into your wedding party games for a sweet (and slightly wild) twist.
