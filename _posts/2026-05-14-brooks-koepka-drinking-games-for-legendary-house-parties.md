## How to Play Brooks Koepka Drinking Games

### 1. Set Up

Get everyone settled in, drinks ready, and the TV tuned to a Brooks Koepka round. Shuffle your dare cards and keep them within easy reach.

### 2. Basic Rules

Whenever any of these things happen during Koepka’s round, the group drinks or completes a dare:

- **Brooks hits a birdie**: Everyone takes two sips. Last person to drink must do a dare challenge.
- **Brooks hits a bogey or worse**: Choose someone to take a shot or finish their drink.
- **Brooks makes the leaderboard**: Group cheers and everyone drinks.
- **Brooks fist pumps or drops a meme-worthy reaction**: NSFW dare for the loudest cheerer.
- **Commentators mention his major wins**: Pass out a dare card to another player.
- **Brooks is shown with his signature swagger**: Last person to shout “Brooks!” drinks twice.

### 3. Dare Challenges

Every missed shot by Brooks = dare time! Draw a card and complete whatever wild challenge comes up. Need ideas? Try:

- Post your best golf meme in the group chat
- Speak in your worst golf commentator voice for the next three minutes
- Attempt a dramatic “Koepka walk” across the room (bonus points for meme potential)
- Down your drink if you can’t do it with a straight face

### 4. Special Rounds

During major tournament moments—like a clutch putt or playoff—everyone has to stand and toast. The player with the emptiest glass gets to assign a dare to anyone in the group.
