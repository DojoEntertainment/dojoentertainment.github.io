## Boston Marathon 2026 Drinking Game Rules

Ready to run the party circuit? Here’s how to play our signature Boston Marathon drinking game:

### The Basics

1. **Divide Into Teams or Go Solo:**  
   Split into groups or play individually. Assign each person or team a color, runner, or country to cheer for.

2. **Set Up Your Drinks:**  
   Everyone keeps their drink close. Hydration is key (even if it’s not water).

3. **Choose Your Dare Challenges:**  
   Use our custom drinking game builder to add spicy NSFW dares or keep it classic with meme drinking games.

### The Main Game

**Drink when:**
- A runner from your team’s country is mentioned.
- The camera zooms in on someone in a wacky costume.
- The announcers say “heartbreak hill.”
- The crowd does something wild (think: streaking, outrageous signs, or viral moments).
- The leader changes.

**Double Dare Challenges:**
- If a runner dramatically falls and gets back up, complete a dare challenge — think planking until the next commercial break or posting a meme-worthy selfie.
- If the commentator mispronounces a runner’s name, the last person to call it out picks an NSFW dare for someone else.

**Viral Drinking Games Add-Ons:**
- First person to spot a meme sign (bonus if it’s about running or drinking) gets to assign two sips.
- If the race coverage shows a marriage proposal, everyone finishes their drink and shares their wildest wedding party games idea.

**Finish Line Bonus Round:**
- When the first runner crosses the finish line, everyone does a group cheers and shares the best house party ideas they’ve seen so far.
- Last to stand up after the winner’s interview picks a dare for the group.
