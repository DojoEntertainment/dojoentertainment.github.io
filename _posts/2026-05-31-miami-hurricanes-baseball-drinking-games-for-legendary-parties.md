## How to Play Miami Hurricanes Baseball Drinking Game

The rules are easy to follow, but the fun? Off the charts. Here’s how you bring the party:

### Basic Rules

1. **Pick Your Players:** Everyone picks a player from the Hurricanes’ starting lineup to “represent” them during the game.
2. **Assign Drinks:** Every time your player does something epic (or embarrassing), you drink. Switch up the rules every inning for extra chaos.
3. **Dare Challenges:** Add a custom dare every time there’s a home run, error, or viral meme moment on the broadcast.

### Sample Drinking Game Rules

- **Every hit:** Take a sip.
- **Strikeout by your player:** Finish your drink.
- **Home run:** Everyone drinks and pulls a meme drinking challenge from the Dare Jar.
- **Pitcher change:** Last person to notice does an NSFW dare.
- **Hurricanes score:** Everyone cheers and drinks.
- **Opponent scores:** Everyone but the fan of the opposing team drinks.
- **Meme-worthy moment on screen:** First person to meme it in the group chat assigns two drinks.

## Next-Level Party Drinking Games Ideas

Don’t stop at the basics! Up the ante with these party-ready twists:
