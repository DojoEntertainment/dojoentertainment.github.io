## Essential Drinking Games for School Closings

These viral drinking games are guaranteed to bring the chaos, laughter, and just enough mayhem to your living room. Here’s what you’ll need and how to play:

### 1. **Snowed-In Meme Drinking Game**

**Materials Needed:**
- Deck of meme cards (DIY or grab a meme pack from [Drinking Dojo](https://drinkingdojo.com))
- Drinks of choice

**How to Play:**
1. Shuffle the meme cards and place them in the center.
2. Each player draws a card on their turn and reads it aloud.
3. If the meme fits someone in the group, that person drinks. If it’s a perfect match, everyone finishes their drink!
4. Add custom dares for your wildest friends—like posting a meme-worthy selfie to your story.

**Why it Rocks:**  
It’s the perfect way to turn online chaos into IRL laughs and unforgettable dares.

### 2. **Custom Dare Challenge Builder**

**Materials Needed:**
- [Custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com)
- Paper slips or a phone to track dares
- Drinks

**How to Play:**
1. Let everyone submit their most savage dare challenges—think NSFW dares, TikTok trends, or school-related chaos.
2. Mix up the dares and draw at random.
3. Complete the dare or take a drink. No backing out!

**Pro Tip:**  
Use the Drinking Dojo custom builder to create a pack themed around your school, city, or favorite inside jokes.

### 3. **Election Day Drinking Game Remix**

Even if it’s not election season, remix this party classic with school-themed prompts.

**Materials Needed:**
- List of school-related events (like “someone mentions the cafeteria,” “a teacher’s catchphrase,” etc.)
- Drinks

**How to Play:**
1. Whenever an event happens, everyone drinks.
2. Last player standing gets to assign a dare to the group.

**Why it Works:**  
It’s a hilarious way to relive your school’s biggest moments—without the homework.
