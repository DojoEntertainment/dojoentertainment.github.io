### Step 1: Pick Your Flick

### Step 2: Choose Your Drinking Game Pack

### Step 3: Set the Rules

Here are some easy drinking challenges to get everyone buzzing:

- **Take a sip** every time a character says a catchphrase or does something cliché.
- **Down your drink** when a plot twist hits out of nowhere.
- **Pass out a dare** (from your custom pack or written on slips of paper) when someone misses a key scene because they’re distracted.
- **Meme Moment:** The first person to spot a cringey or iconic meme-worthy moment picks who drinks next.

Want more structure? Try these custom rules for popular genres:

#### Action Movie Drinking Game

- Every explosion or chase scene: **1 sip**
- Main character dodges death: **2 sips**
- Villain laughs maniacally: **Give a dare**

#### Rom-Com Drinking Game

- Awkward kiss or confession: **1 sip**
- Best friend gives questionable advice: **Give a dare**
- Predictable ending: **Everyone finishes their drink**

#### Horror Movie Drinking Game

- Someone goes into the basement alone: **1 sip**
- Jump scare: **2 sips**
- Character makes a terrible decision: **Give a dare**
