## How to Play the Daily Horoscope Drinking Game

### 1. Get Your Signs

- Everyone announces their star sign. If you don’t know yours, Google has your back.

### 2. Horoscope Check

- Each player checks their daily horoscope (Astrology apps, Twitter, or wherever you get your daily fix).

### 3. Spot the Drinking Triggers

- Listen for keywords. Every time your horoscope mentions a word from the list below, you drink—or assign drinks!

  **Take a sip if your horoscope mentions:**
  - Luck or opportunity
  - Love or romance
  - Change or transformation
  - Adventure or travel
  - Challenge or obstacle

  **Take two sips if your horoscope mentions:**
  - Mystery or secrets
  - Drama
  - Success or victory

### 4. Dare Challenges

- If your horoscope is super vague (think “something good might happen”), you draw a dare card or use Drinking Dojo’s meme drinking games pack for an NSFW dare. Complete it—or drink!

### 5. Star Sign Showdown

- At the end of a round, everyone votes on the “spiciest” horoscope. That player becomes the “Celestial Commander” and gets to make a rule for the next round (think: “No saying the word ‘drink’” or “Everyone must toast before sipping”).
