### 1. Bracket Busters

**Materials:**
- Your bracket (printed or digital)
- Drinks of choice (beer, cocktails, hard seltzers, whatever your crew loves)
- Shot glasses

**Rules:**
- Everyone fills out a bracket before the semifinals tip off.
- Every time a pick gets busted (eliminated), take a sip.
- If your championship team loses, finish your drink.
- Last person with a still-alive bracket picks a dare challenge for another player.

**How to Play:**  
Keep score of bracket losses and get ready for some wild NSFW dares if you’re feeling bold.

### 2. Final 4 Bingo Bash

**Materials:**
- Pre-made basketball bingo cards (make your own or grab them from a drinking game builder)
- Drinks
- Markers or pens

**Rules:**
- Fill the bingo cards with game events (e.g., “coach throws a tantrum,” “player misses free throw,” “crowd goes wild,” “meme-worthy moment”).
- When something on your card happens, take a sip and mark the square.
- First to get bingo chooses a dare challenge for anyone—or everyone—at the party.

**How to Play:**  
Perfect for house party ideas, especially if your crew loves viral drinking games and meme drinking games.

### 3. Shot Clock Showdown

**Materials:**
- Timer (your phone works)
- Drinks
- Dare challenge cards (or create your own with a meme twist)

**Rules:**
- Every time the shot clock winds down to under 5 seconds, everyone drinks.
- If a team scores at the buzzer, all fans of the opposing team must complete a dare.
- For couples drinking games, team up and double the dares if your chosen team is behind at halftime.

**How to Play:**  
Crank up the heat with NSFW dares or keep it chill with goofy, meme-inspired challenges.
