### Drinking Game Prompts

Take a sip when:
- A runner is shown grabbing water at a hydration station
- The announcers mention the weather
- You see a spectator in a ridiculous costume
- Someone trips or stumbles (be nice—just a sip!)
- They cut to a commercial break

Take two sips when:
- A runner overtakes the leader
- The camera zooms in on a funny sign
- There’s a dog in the crowd
- The announcer tries to pronounce a tricky name

Finish your drink or take a shot when:
- A runner sets a new course record
- The lead changes in the final mile
- Someone proposes at the finish line (it’s more common than you think!)
- Your chosen runner finishes the race

### Dare Challenges and Meme Drinking Games

Spice things up with NSFW dares or meme drinking games:
- If you spot a meme-worthy moment, challenge another player to create a quick meme or TikTok. Loser drinks!
- Draw a dare from your bowl—think “Post a selfie with a marathon pose” or “Do a lap around the living room.”
- Couples drinking games: If you’re playing as a pair, every time your runner is mentioned, switch drinks with your partner.
