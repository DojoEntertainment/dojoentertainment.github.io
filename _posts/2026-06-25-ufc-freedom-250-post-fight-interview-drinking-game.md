## How to Play

### The Basic Rules

1. **Gather your crew** (2+ players). Pick teams if you want to add rivalry.
2. **Tune in** to the UFC Freedom 250 post-fight interview.
3. **Follow the drink cues below** every time they happen during the interview.
4. **Add dares and meme drinking games** to spice things up between questions.

### Drink Cues

Take a sip (or a shot if you’re feeling bold) every time:

- The fighter thanks their “team” or “coaches”
- Someone says, “I just want to thank my fans”
- The interviewer asks, “What’s next for you?”
- A fighter calls out a rival or starts trash-talking
- There’s an awkward silence or a technical glitch
- The crowd starts booing or cheering loudly
- The fighter mentions their country or hometown

**Bonus round:**  
Whenever anyone says “freedom” or references the event name, everyone finishes their drink!
