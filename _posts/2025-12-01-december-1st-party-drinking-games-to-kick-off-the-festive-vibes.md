## Top Party Drinking Games for December 1st

Ready to make December 1st legendary? Here are some of the best party drinking games, plus a few wild dare challenges and meme drinking games to keep the vibe going strong.

### 1. Meme Roulette: Viral Drinking Games at Their Best

**Perfect for:** Anyone who lives for viral drinking games and meme culture.

**How to play:**
- Gather everyone in a circle and pass around your phone, each player must find a meme (funniest wins).
- The group votes for the best meme.
- The loser? They take a drink and must complete a dare challenge from the Drinking Dojo custom dare pack.

**Materials:** Smartphones, a sense of humor, and dare cards (or use [Drinking Dojo’s custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com)).

### 2. December Dare Jenga: House Party Ideas with a Twist

**Perfect for:** House party ideas with a twist.

**How to play:**
- Write NSFW dares and classic drinking challenges on Jenga blocks.
- Each player pulls a block and does what it says—or takes two drinks if they chicken out.

**Materials:** Jenga set, marker, and your wildest imagination.

### 3. Couples Drinking Games: Naughty or Nice for Date Nights

**Perfect for:** Date nights or spicy group hangouts.

**How to play:**
- Couples (or paired-up friends) draw cards—one “naughty,” one “nice.”
- Naughty = complete a dare challenge.
- Nice = give out drinks to other players.

**Materials:** Dare cards, drink of choice, and a playful mood.

### 4. Fortnite Drinking Game: Drinking Challenges Meets Gaming

**Perfect for:** Gamers and party animals alike.

**How to play:**
- Play a round of Fortnite (or your favorite battle royale).
- Every elimination = take a sip.
- If you’re first to get knocked out, finish your drink and do a meme-worthy dare challenge.

**Materials:** Console/PC, drinks, and an appetite for chaos.

### 5. Inauguration Day Drinking Game Remix: Election Day Drinking Game with a Political Twist

**Perfect for:** Adding a twist to December 1st with some political spice.

**How to play:**
- Pick a recent inauguration speech or election day highlight reel.
- Assign common phrases or actions (like “unity,” “promise,” or applause) to drinks.
- When it happens, everyone drinks. If someone misses their cue, they face a dare challenge.

**Materials:** TV/laptop, drinks, and a list of phrases.
