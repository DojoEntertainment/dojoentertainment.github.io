## What You Need

- **Plastic cups** (at least one per player, plus one for the “center”)
- **A large table**
- **Alcohol of choice** (beer, cocktails, etc.)
- **A deck of cards** (optional for extra dares)
- **Friends who love viral drinking games**

## How to Play the James Bond Drinking Game

Ready to get the squad together for some meme drinking games magic? Here’s how the James Bond drinking game works:

### Setup

1. **Arrange Cups:** Fill the center cup with any drink (the stronger, the better for dare challenges).
2. **Everyone Gets a Cup:** Each player has their own cup, filled with their beverage.
3. **Sit Around the Table:** Make sure everyone can reach the center cup.

### The Rules

1. **Start the Action:** Someone yells “James Bond!” to kick off the round.
2. **Race to Stack:** Everyone races to stack their cup into the center cup, one by one, as fast as possible.
3. **Last One Loses:** The last person to stack their cup is the “loser” and must drink the center cup.
4. **Dare Challenges:** Want more mayhem? Use a deck of cards: before drinking, the loser draws a card. Each suit represents a different dare—hearts for NSFW dares, clubs for drinking challenges, spades for meme-worthy actions, and diamonds for classic party dares.
5. **Keep It Moving:** After each round, refill the center cup, and start again!

### House Rules and Custom Twists

- **Meme Mode:** Add in viral drinking games rules—anyone who references a Bond movie line can hand out a drink.
- **Wedding Party Games Twist:** For wedding ragers, swap drinks for champagne and use romantic dares.
- **Couples Drinking Games Version:** Partners play as a team and face couple-themed dares or challenges.
