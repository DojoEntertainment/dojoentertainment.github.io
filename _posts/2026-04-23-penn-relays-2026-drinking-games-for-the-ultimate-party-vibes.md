## The Penn Relays 2026 Drinking Game: Rules, Setup, and How To Play

Here’s how you turn the legendary event into a legendary party:

### What You’ll Need

- Your favorite drinks (beer, cocktails, mocktails—whatever fits your vibe)
- A TV or livestream of Penn Relays 2026
- Printed or digital list of dare challenges
- Optional: meme cards, custom rule cards, and your wildest friends

### Game Rules

1. **Assign Teams:**  
   Break up into teams (just like the relays). If you’re feeling wild, make teams based on your favorite universities or countries competing.

2. **Race Round:**  
   Every time a new relay race starts, everyone takes a sip.  
   *Bonus:* If your team’s school is racing, double up!

3. **False Start Fiasco:**  
   If there’s a false start, everyone has to do a dare challenge drawn from the pile—think NSFW dares or meme drinking games (keep it spicy, but safe).

4. **Record Breaker:**  
   Whenever a record is smashed, finish your drink and high-five the room.  
   *Optional:* The person who predicted the winner picks someone to complete a dare.

5. **Track Memes:**  
   Spot a viral moment (crazy outfits, epic finish-line faces, wild celebrations)? The last person to notice or meme it takes a shot.

6. **Relay Switch:**  
   During every baton handoff, switch drinks with the person on your right. If someone fumbles their glass, they must complete a drinking challenge.

7. **Victory Lap:**  
   When your team wins, everyone else raises a toast and drinks in your honor.  
   *Losing team?* Prepare for a group dare!

### Dare Challenges

- Share your best Penn Relays meme in the group chat.
- Attempt your own living room relay—winner picks someone to drink.
- Do your best commentator impression.
- Post a selfie doing a victory pose with your drink.
- Take on a viral drinking game from [Drinking Dojo’s custom builder](https://drinkingdojo.com).
