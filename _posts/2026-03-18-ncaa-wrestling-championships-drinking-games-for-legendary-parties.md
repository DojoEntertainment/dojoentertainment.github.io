## NCAA Wrestling Championships Drinking Game Rules

### Basic Rules For Party Drinking Games

Here’s a simple way to play—just assign drinks to big moments during the matches. Start with these, or use the [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) to make your own:

- **Take 1 sip** every time a wrestler scores a takedown
- **Take 2 sips** for every reversal or escape
- **Finish your drink** if there’s a pin!
- **Pass out a drink** to someone else if the ref calls a controversial point
- **Do a dare challenge** (like a meme drinking game twist) if the crowd goes wild or a coach loses it on the sidelines

### Dare Challenges And Viral Meme Drinking Games

Want to add more chaos? Toss in these viral drinking game dares:

- **NSFW Dare:** If a wrestler rips their singlet, the last person to notice has to do an NSFW dare
- **Meme Moment:** When the announcers make a weird analogy, everyone must come up with their own meme caption—best one gets to hand out drinks
- **Viral Replay:** If a play goes viral on Twitter or TikTok during the event, everyone does a group shot

## House Party Ideas For Your NCAA Wrestling Watch Party

- **Couples Drinking Games:** Pair up and compete—loser does a dare challenge
- **Scorekeeper Role:** Assign a “ref” to keep track of sips, dares, and meme moments
