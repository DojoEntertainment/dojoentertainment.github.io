## What Is the American Dream Drinking Game?

The American Dream Drinking Game is all about chasing the wildest fun with your friends, fueled by creative dare challenges and meme drinking games. It’s designed for groups who want to bend the rules, laugh at the chaos, and maybe reveal a secret talent or two (karaoke, anyone?). ## What You’ll Need

- **A deck of cards** (standard or custom, like those from Drinking Dojo)
- **Solo cups or glasses** for each player
- **Your favorite drinks** (alcoholic or non-alcoholic—your call)
- **A phone** for dares and meme challenges
- **A playlist** packed with bangers (bonus points for American classics)

## How To Play

### Setup

1. **Circle Up**: Everyone sits in a circle with their drink.
2. **Deal the Cards**: Shuffle the deck and place it in the middle.
3. **Choose a Dream Team Leader**: The person who last saw a bald eagle (or just the birthday person) starts.

### Card Rules

Draw a card and follow the action below. If you need inspiration for dares or memes, check out [Drinking Dojo’s custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) for epic ideas.

- **Ace**: America the Brave  
  Make a toast about your boldest dream. Everyone drinks to freedom.

- **2**: Dare to Dream  
  The drawer gives a dare challenge to another player. Refuse? Take two drinks.

- **3**: Meme Supreme  
  Show your funniest meme in the group chat. Best reaction picks someone to drink.

- **4**: Party for the People  
  Everyone drinks if they’ve ever been to an American diner.

- **5**: Challenge the System  
  Start a drinking challenge—who can do the best Statue of Liberty pose? Loser drinks.

- **6**: Election Day  
  Nominate two players for a quick-fire debate (on something ridiculous). Group votes for winner; loser drinks.

- **7**: NSFW Liberty  
  Draw an NSFW dare from the Dojo pack or make one up. Refuse? Double drink.

- **8**: Fortnite Dance Break  
  Do your best Fortnite dance. Too shy? Take a drink.

- **9**: Inauguration Toast  
  Everyone says what they’d do as president. Most outrageous idea makes someone drink.

- **10**: Couples Constitution  
  Pair up for a couples drinking game mini-challenge (like truth or dare, or a silly contest).

- **J, Q, K**: House Party Heroes  
  Start a group drinking challenge—first to finish wins glory, last place drinks twice.
