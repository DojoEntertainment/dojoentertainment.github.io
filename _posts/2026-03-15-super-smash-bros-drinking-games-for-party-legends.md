## How To Play Super Smash Bros Drinking Games

### Basic Rules

The aim? Win the match or survive the chaos with your dignity (sort of) intact. Here’s a simple format to get started:

1. **Pick Your Characters**: Everyone selects their fighter. If you want to spice it up, assign random characters each round.
2. **Set The Stakes**: Before each match, agree on the rules for drinks—like sips, gulps, or shots—for certain in-game events.
3. **Play A Match**: Classic free-for-all or teams, depending on your group size.
4. **Drink Up**: Take drinks based on what happens in the game (see ideas below).
5. **Dare Challenges**: Losers pull a dare from the Drinking Dojo pack or your custom builder. Get ready for some viral drinking game moments.

### Drinking Rules Ideas

Mix and match these to fit your party vibe:

- **Take a sip when…**
  - You get KO’d (knocked off the stage)
  - You self-destruct (fall off by accident)
  - Your special move whiffs completely
- **Take a big gulp if…**
  - You’re the first to lose all your lives
  - You get hit by a Final Smash
- **Finish your drink if…**
  - You end the match with zero KOs
- **Dare time if…**
  - You’re last place—draw a dare challenge from the [Drinking Dojo custom game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) or NSFW dares pack

### House Rules & Extra Twists

Want to really heat things up? Add these viral drinking game twists:

- **Character Roulette**: Randomly assign fighters each round—no mains allowed!
- **Meme Mode**: Play as meme-worthy characters only (looking at you, Kirby).
- **Couples Showdown**: Partners team up. The losing couple faces a couples drinking game dare.
- **Wedding Party Games Version**: Winner chooses who in the group takes an extra drink—great for stag or hen dos.
