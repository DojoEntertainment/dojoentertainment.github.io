- A TV or stream showing the live US Open leaderboard (or use a live scoreboard site on your phone)

## How to Play

**1. Choose Your Champion(s):**
- Each player picks one golfer from the US Open leaderboard before the round or session starts. For smaller groups, you can double up or assign two golfers per player.
- If you want to spice things up, use our [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) to assign meme-inspired nicknames or wild dares to each golfer.

**2. Leaderboard Sips:**
- Every time your golfer moves up on the leaderboard, give out a sip to another player.
- If your golfer drops, you take a sip.
- If your golfer hits a birdie (one under par), everyone cheers and drinks.
- If your golfer bogeys (one over par), you choose someone to draw from the Dare Challenge deck.

**3. Dare Challenges and Viral Twists:**
- At the end of each hole, whoever’s golfer has the worst score must draw a dare card (think NSFW dares for the brave or viral meme drinking games for laughs).
- Anyone with a golfer who’s tied for the lead must toast and finish their drink.

**4. Extra Drinking Challenges:**
- Hole-in-One: Everyone downs their drink!
- Water Hazard: If your golfer lands in the water, you perform a dare and take a shot.
- Leaderboard Flip: If there’s a new leader, everyone swaps seats and drinks with the person to their left.

**5. Wedding Party Games or Couples Drinking Game Variant:**
- Playing at a wedding or with couples? Pair up and combine scores. If your team’s golfers both hit par or better, hand out dares to another team. If not, perform a group challenge!
