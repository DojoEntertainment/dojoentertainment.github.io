## How to Play the US Open 2026 Cut Line Drinking Game

### Game Prep

1. **Pick Your Players**: Everyone claims a golfer in the field—draw names or pick your favorites.
2. **Set Your Cut Line**: Announce the projected cut line before the second round begins. (This is usually around the top 60 and ties, but varies each year.)
3. **Make Dare Cards**: Toss in a few meme drinking games or **NSFW dares** for anyone who gets too cocky about their golfer making the cut.

### Game Rules

#### Sip When:
- Your golfer bogeys
- Announcer mentions “cut line”
- Any player lands in the bunker

#### Take a Big Gulp When:
- Your golfer hits water or OB
- The leaderboard shows the projected cut line moving
- A fan yells something ridiculous on TV

#### Dare Challenge:
- If your golfer is below the cut line at the turn (after 9 holes), you must draw a dare from the pile. This keeps the action spicy and adds a **viral drinking game** vibe.

#### Finish Your Drink If:
- Your golfer misses the cut. (Ouch!)
- Someone’s golfer eagles the final hole and saves their spot—choose someone to join you in a celebratory chug.

#### Bonus Round: Meme Drinking Game
- Create a group meme about the most epic fail or wild shot and post it in your group chat for extra points. Loser does a shot!

### Couples or Wedding Version

Hosting a **wedding party** or double date during the tournament? Just split into teams or couples. The losing team after the cut line must complete a dare together—bonus points for creativity. This twist also works for **house party ideas** or if you’re just vibing with mates in the dorm.
