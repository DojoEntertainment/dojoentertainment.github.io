Ready to get wild? Here are some crowd-favorite drinking games to play using the latest siri app features.

### 1. Siri Says: Dare Challenge Edition

Think Simon Says, but with more shots and less shame.

**How to Play:**
- Gather your group and your drinks of choice.
- Say, “Hey Siri, start Dare Challenge.”
- Siri will call out dares or challenges. If you’re too slow or fail, take a sip (or a shot).
- You can customize dares to be tame or totally NSFW.
- First to 5 fails must do a group-chosen dare.

**Materials Needed:**
- iPhone with ios 27 and Siri enabled
- Drinks
- Your bravest friends

### 2. Meme Drinking Game Powered by Siri

Ever wanted to play a meme drinking game? Now you can, hands-free.

**How to Play:**
- Everyone submits their favorite meme themes to Siri (think: “Distracted Boyfriend,” “Woman Yelling at Cat,” etc.).
- Siri randomly assigns memes and drinking rules (e.g., “Whenever someone mentions ‘vibe check,’ everyone sips”).
- Bonus: Ask Siri to pull up viral meme soundbites for extra laughs.

**Materials Needed:**
- iPhone with ios 27
- Drinks
- Meme-loving crew

### 3. Custom Couples Drinking Games

Couples night? Siri has your back with custom games.

**How to Play:**
- Each couple tells Siri their favorite in-jokes or relationship quirks.
- Siri creates a personalized challenge list (e.g., “If your partner leaves dirty dishes, take a shot!”).
- Compete for the title of Ultimate Drinking Dojo Couple.

**Materials Needed:**
- iPhone with ios 27
- Drinks
- Couples ready for some fun
