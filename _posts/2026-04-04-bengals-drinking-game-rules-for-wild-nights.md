## What You Need for Bengals

- 1 standard deck of cards  
- At least 3 players (the more, the wilder)  
- Plenty of drinks (beer, cocktails, whatever you love)  
- A table or flat surface  
- Dare challenges or **NSFW dares** (optional, but highly recommended for extra chaos)

## How to Play Bengals

### Setup

1. Shuffle the deck of cards and place it facedown in the center.  
2. Everyone grabs their drink and sits around the table.  
3. Decide who goes first (oldest, youngest, wildest dancer—your pick).

### Game Play

Bengals is all about keeping your head in the game and your drink in hand. Here’s how it goes down:

1. **Draw a Card:** On your turn, draw the top card from the pile.  
2. **Call Out a Number:** Before you flip the card, shout out “Bengal!” and guess a number between 2 and 10. (Face cards count as 10, Aces are 1 or 11—you pick!)  
3. **Reveal:** Flip the card. If your guess matches the card number, everyone else drinks. If you’re wrong, you take a drink.  
4. **Dare Time:** If you draw a face card, you have two options:  
    - Take a big drink  
    - Complete a dare challenge picked by the group. (This is where **NSFW dares** or **meme drinking games** get spicy!)  
5. **Bengal Streak:** If someone guesses correctly three times in a row, they become the “Bengal Master.” The Bengal Master can invent a new rule for the group—get creative!  
6. **End of Round:** Keep going until the deck runs out or everyone’s out of drinks.
