## Tampa Weather Watch Drinking Game

This one’s simple, wild, and works whether you’re glued to the weather app or just peeking out the window.

### What You Need

- A group of friends (the more, the better)
- Drinks of your choice
- Access to a weather app or weather channel
- A list of weather-related dares (keep reading for ideas!)

### How To Play

1. **Set Up:** Everyone grabs a drink and tunes in to the real-time Tampa weather report (on your phone or TV).
2. **Choose Your Forecast:** Before the game starts, each person bets on the *next* weather change (rain, sun, wind, thunder, etc.).
3. **React To The Weather:** When the weather changes, everyone who guessed wrong takes a drink.
4. **Dare Challenges:** For every weather alert (like a thunderclap, heavy rain, or unexpected sunshine), draw a dare from your custom dare challenge list. Think “sing a weather-themed song” or “do your best impression of a hurricane.”
5. **Meme Moments:** Snap a selfie or short video every time the weather makes you react big—share with the group or on your story for ultimate meme drinking game points.

## Weather Roulette Dare Game

Channel that unpredictable Tampa weather energy into a fast-paced dare challenge.

### What You Need

- Deck of cards or a custom spinner (use our [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) for next-level fun)
- Drinks
- List of NSFW dares (if you’re feeling bold), plus weather-themed dares

### How To Play

1. **Everyone Draws:** Players take turns drawing a card or spinning.
2. **Match The Forecast:** Each card or spinner number matches a weather scenario (e.g., Ace = lightning round, 2 = sprinkle, 3 = sun, etc.)
3. **Complete The Dare:** The player must complete a dare based on the weather scenario. For example, “Sun” might mean you have to wear sunglasses indoors for the next three rounds. “Lightning” could mean you have to chug your drink.
4. **Refuse A Dare?** Take two sips instead!

Get creative with the dares—think “pretend to be a weather reporter,” “make up your own Tampa forecast,” or go NSFW with couples drinking games if the group is up for it.

## Rain Delay Challenge

When rain ruins your outdoor plans, turn the delay into a viral drinking game.

### What You Need

- Drinks
- Timer or stopwatch
- Weather app
- Fun house party ideas

### How To Play

1. **Start The Timer:** Every time the rain starts, set a 5-minute timer.
2. **Challenge Time:** At the end of every 5 minutes, draw a dare or complete a mini drinking challenge. Last person to finish their drink must do the next dare.
3. **Keep It Going:** If the rain keeps pouring, so does the game!
