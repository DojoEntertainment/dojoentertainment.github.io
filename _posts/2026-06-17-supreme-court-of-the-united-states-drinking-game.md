## What You Need

- A group of friends (the more, the merrier!)
- Drinks of your choice (beer, cocktails, mocktails—judge’s orders)
- A TV or device to stream Supreme Court hearings, news, or meme clips
- Printed or digital list of rules and dare challenges
- Optional: Robes, wigs, and gavels for bonus vibes

## How to Play

This game mixes classic dare challenges, meme drinking games, and viral drinking game elements to keep everyone on their toes. Here’s how to get started:

### Step 1: Choose Your Justices

Assign each player the name of a Supreme Court Justice or let everyone pick their own title (Chief Justice, Associate Justice, etc.).

### Step 2: Set the Scene

Start streaming a Supreme Court hearing, news segment, or viral meme compilation about the court. If you can’t find a live event, pick a themed documentary or YouTube highlight reel.

### Step 3: Drink When…

Each time one of these classic moments happens, take a sip or complete a dare:

- **Someone says “precedent,” “ruling,” or “petition”** — 1 drink
- **A justice interrupts another speaker** — 1 drink, last to notice does a dare
- **A lawyer says “Your Honor”** — 1 drink, everyone cheers
- **A meme-worthy facial expression happens** — 2 drinks, best impression wins immunity for the round
- **Technical difficulties or awkward silence** — 2 drinks, everyone must speak in legal jargon for 1 minute

### Step 4: Dare Challenges

Spice it up with these viral drinking game dares:

- **Gavel Drop**: If anyone drops their drink or knocks something over, they must argue why they shouldn’t have to drink (group votes on their fate).
- **Objection Overruled**: When someone shouts “Objection!” at any point, everyone points at another player—the slowest must take 2 drinks.
- **NSFW Dares**: For the bold, add in some spicy NSFW dares (keep it friendly and fun—consent is key!).

### Step 5: The Verdict

At the end of the session, everyone votes for the Most Dramatic Justice, the Meme King/Queen, and the Best Legal Argument. Winners get bragging rights or a pass on the next round of drinks.
