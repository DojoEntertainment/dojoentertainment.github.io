Plus, when your favorite creators show off the latest custom drinking game builder or dare challenges, you know it’s about to get unforgettable.

Here’s why influencer-driven drinking games are blowing up:
- **Instant virality:** One post, and suddenly everyone’s playing a Fortnite drinking game or trying spicy meme drinking games.
- **Real reactions:** Influencers don’t hold back—they show the chaos, the laughter, and the epic fails.
- **Creative twists:** Influencers are always remixing classic party drinking games or inventing their own, so you never run out of house party ideas.

### How to Play Like an Influencer

Ready to host a party that could go viral? Here’s how you can use influencer marketing inspo to turn your next drinking game night into a legendary event.

#### 1. Pick Your Game

Start with games that are already trending, like:
- **Viral drinking games:** Games you’ve seen blowing up on social media. Try Drinking Dojo’s meme drinking games for instant laughs.
- **Custom packs:** Use our custom drinking game builder to create challenges tailored for your crew—perfect for couples drinking games or a wild wedding party.
- **Themed games:** Celebrate big events with a Fortnite drinking game, an inauguration day drinking game, or even an election day drinking game.

#### 2. Gather Your Materials

- **Drinks of choice:** Beer, seltzers, cocktails, or mocktails—whatever fits your vibe.
- **Game cards or app:** Use Drinking Dojo’s packs or digital tools for easy setup.
- **Props:** Some dares get wild, so grab silly hats, face paint, or whatever else you need.
- **Phone or camera:** Capture the chaos (and maybe become a viral influencer yourself).

#### 3. Set the Rules

- **Follow the game pack instructions**—or make up your own using the custom builder.
- For **dare challenges**, stack up NSFW dares or meme-inspired tasks.
- Decide on the number of turns, rounds, or drinks per challenge.
- Always have a “water break” rule—hydration is key!

#### 4. Share the Fun

- Snap pics, record videos, and post them with your own party hashtag.
- Tag Drinking Dojo and your favorite creators—maybe your party becomes the next viral drinking game trend!
- For extra spice, let guests suggest dares or meme moments for the next round.

### Pro Tips for Influencer-Worthy Drinking Games

- **Keep it inclusive:** Make sure everyone is comfortable with the dares and challenges.
- **Switch it up:** Rotate between games like couples drinking games, meme drinking games, and wild house party ideas.
- **Safety first:** Know your limits and look out for your mates.
