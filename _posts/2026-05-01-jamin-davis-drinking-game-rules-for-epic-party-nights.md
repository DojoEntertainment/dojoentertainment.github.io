## How To Play The Jamin Davis Drinking Game

Ready for action? Here’s how to get started:

### 1. Set Up Your Crew

Gather everyone in one spot and make sure everyone’s got a drink in hand. Decide if you want to keep it chill or go full-throttle with wild dare challenges.

### 2. Turn On The Game

Stream any game featuring Jamin Davis (NFL games are ideal, especially if you’re vibing with football fans). For those not into sports, you can adapt the rules to fit meme drinking games—just swap in your favorite viral clips or highlight reels.

### 3. The Rules

Take a drink every time…

- **Jamin Davis makes a tackle**: Sip up!
- **His name is mentioned on the broadcast**: Everyone drinks.
- **He gets a highlight replay**: Double shots all around.
- **He celebrates or dances**: Dare challenge time! The group picks a player to do a meme-worthy dance or take an NSFW dare.
- **The commentator makes a pun about his name**: Last person to notice takes two drinks.
