## How To Play the Donny Osmond Drinking Game

This isn’t your grandma’s party—unless your grandma loves dare challenges and meme drinking games. Here’s how to make Donny the star of your next house party.

### Setup

1. **Create a Playlist:** Load up a mix of Donny Osmond classics and a few wild cards for surprise rounds.
2. **Circle Up:** Sit in a circle so everyone’s got a front-row seat to the action.
3. **Designate a Game Master:** This person keeps the rules flowing and the dares wild.

### Basic Rules

#### Take a Sip When:

- Donny hits a high note
- Someone sings along (bonus sip if they actually know the lyrics)
- Anyone mentions “Puppy Love”
- There’s a costume change in a music video

#### Take Two Sips If:

- Someone confesses they’ve never heard a Donny Osmond song before
- A meme-worthy dance move happens
- The Game Master shouts “Donny Time!”

#### Dare Challenges

Throw in a card from the dare pile when:

- The same song plays twice in a row
- Someone accidentally calls him “Danny” or “Johnny”
- The room bursts into laughter at a meme

A few NSFW dares or meme-inspired challenges can push the party to legendary status (just keep it safe, folks).

#### Shot Round

Whenever “Go Away Little Girl” plays, everyone takes a shot. No excuses.
