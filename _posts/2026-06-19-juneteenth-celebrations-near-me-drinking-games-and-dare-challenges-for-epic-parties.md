## The Ultimate Drinking Games for Juneteenth Parties

### 1. **Freedom Toast**

A party drinking game that’s all about raising your glass to the victories—big and small.

**How to Play:**
- Everyone sits in a circle with their drink of choice.
- One person starts by making a toast (serious or silly) to something worth celebrating.
- Everyone drinks after each toast.
- The next person continues with a new toast.
- If someone repeats a toast or freezes under pressure, they face a dare challenge.

**You’ll Need:**
- Drinks for everyone
- List of dares (keep them spicy with NSFW dares if your crew is up for it!)

### 2. **Meme Drinking Game Showdown**

Bring the best meme drinking games energy to your Juneteenth party—perfect for groups who love to laugh.

**How to Play:**
- Print out or pull up a bunch of viral memes on your phone.
- Take turns showing a meme to the group.
- If it gets a laugh, everyone drinks. No laughs? The presenter takes two sips and does a dare.
- Up the ante with dare challenges themed around classic Juneteenth moments or inside jokes.

**You’ll Need:**
- A phone or laptop
- A collection of memes (or use a meme generator app)
- Your favorite drinks

### 3. **Custom Dare Builder Challenge**

Want a Juneteenth party that stands out? Use a custom drinking game builder to create a personalized deck of dares, trivia, and challenges celebrating Black history, music, and culture.

**How to Play:**
- Before the party, use [Drinking Dojo’s custom builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) to create a deck or print out cards.
- Mix questions (e.g., “Name a Black artist who changed music forever”) with dares (dance-off to Beyoncé, recite a poem, or try a traditional dish).
- Players draw cards and must complete the challenge—or take a drink!

**You’ll Need:**
- Custom cards (digital or printed)
- Drinks and props for dares (think: music, snacks, costumes)
