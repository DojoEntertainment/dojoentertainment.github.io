## How To Play: MLB Trade Rumors Drinking Game Rules

### 1. Set Up the Rumor Mill

Everyone grabs a drink and pulls up the latest MLB trade rumors on their favorite device. Assign one person as the “Rumor Referee” to read out updates as they come in.

### 2. Take a Sip If…

- Your team is mentioned in a rumor  
- There’s a “mystery team” in the mix  
- An analyst calls a trade “blockbuster”  
- Someone references a player’s old stats  
- The phrase “pending physical” pops up  

### 3. Take a Big Gulp If…

- A trade actually happens while you’re playing  
- A player gets traded for “cash considerations”  
- A rumor is immediately debunked live  
- The same player is mentioned in three different rumors in one session

### 4. Dare Challenges and Meme Drinking Game Moves

Every time a trade is announced, everyone draws a dare card or spins a challenge from the Drinking Dojo custom drinking game builder. Example dares:

- Post a wild (but safe for work) meme about the trade on your Insta story  
- Make up a fake trade rumor in your best commentator voice  
- Take a shot while singing a team’s theme song  
- For the brave: NSFW dares if your group’s into that

### 5. Wild Party Drinking Games Bonus Rounds

Keep your party energy high with these viral twists:

- **Fortnite drinking game crossover:** If a trade involves a player with a Fortnite celebration, everyone does a dance and drinks.
- **Election day drinking game vibes:** Predict the next trade like it’s an election result—winner gets to assign drinks or a dare.
