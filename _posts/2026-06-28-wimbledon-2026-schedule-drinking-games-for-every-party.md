## How To Play: Wimbledon 2026 Drinking Game Rules

### The Basics

1. **Pick Your Players:** Solo, doubles, or a whole squad—everyone’s in.
2. **Set Up:** Everyone gets their drink and a spot close to the screen.
3. **Choose Your Triggers:** Use the schedule to pick specific matches or play along with every game.

### Drinking Game Triggers

- **Ace Alert:** Whenever a player serves an ace, everyone takes a sip.
- **Double Fault:** Last person to raise their glass does a dare challenge.
- **Challenge Accepted:** When a Hawkeye challenge is used, finish your drink if the call is overturned.
- **Break Point:** When a break of serve happens, the group does a group dare or meme drinking game.
- **Epic Rally:** Any rally over 10 shots? Last person to shout “Let!” must take two sips.
- **Royal Sighting:** Spot someone in the Royal Box? Everyone drinks!
