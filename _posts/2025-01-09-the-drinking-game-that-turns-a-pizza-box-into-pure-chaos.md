### How to Play

**Setup**

1. Place the pizza box in the middle of the group.
2. Everyone writes their name somewhere on the box and draws a circle around it.
3. Decide one important rule upfront: *What happens if the coin hits a line?* (Most groups allow a re-throw.)

**Gameplay**

1. Players take turns tossing the coin onto the box.
2. What happens next depends on where it lands:

- **On a name:** That person drinks.
- **On an empty spot:** You create a new rule, write it down, and circle it.
- **On an existing rule:** You must follow the rule immediately. No arguing.

The game continues until the box is full‚Äîor until the group decides things have gone far enough.
