## What Are Real World Drinking Games?

Real world drinking games are all about mixing classic party drinking games with challenges inspired by actual places, events, and trends. From meme drinking games to viral dares, these games turn your party into a full-on experience—no boring moments allowed.

## The Ultimate Real World Drinking Game: House Party Edition

### What You Need

- Your favorite drinks (shots, beer, cocktails—whatever fuels the fun)
- Cards or slips of paper
- A phone with internet access (for meme or viral challenges)
- A fearless crew ready for anything

### Setting Up

1. Write down a mix of dares, meme references, and drinking challenges on cards. Throw in some wild NSFW dares if your group is up for it.
2. Shuffle the cards and put them in a pile at the center.
3. Everyone sits in a circle with their drink in hand.

### How to Play

1. **Draw & Dare:** On your turn, draw a card and read the challenge out loud.  
2. **Complete or Drink:** You must complete the dare or take the number of sips/shots written on the card.
3. **Real World Challenges:** Mix in specific events—like “Election Day Debate: Argue with another player for 30 seconds, loser drinks,” or “Fortnite Dance-Off: Bust your best move, judge decides who drinks.”
4. **Meme Moment:** If a card says “Meme Challenge,” the group picks a trending meme, and you must act it out in your own way. Winner picks who drinks next.
5. **Wedding Vibes:** For wedding party games, add cards like “Propose to someone in the room. If they say no, drink twice!”
6. **Couples Twist:** For couples drinking games, draw a “Couple’s Challenge” card—like “Share a secret or both drink.”

Keep going until everyone’s feeling the good vibes—or until the cards run out!
