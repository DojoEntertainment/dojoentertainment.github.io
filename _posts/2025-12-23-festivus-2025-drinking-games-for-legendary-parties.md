## The Ultimate Festivus Drinking Game

### Set Up

1. Gather everyone around the Festivus pole (or a DIY stand-in).
2. Shuffle your **dare challenge** cards or open up [Drinking Dojo’s custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) to create a Festivus-themed deck.
3. Decide who goes first (oldest grievance, youngest player, or whoever has the wildest Festivus sweater).

### How to Play

#### Round 1: Airing of Grievances

- **Rules**: Each player “airs a grievance” about someone or something in the room. The group votes on whether it’s valid or a stretch.
- **Drinking Challenge**: If your grievance is ruled a stretch, take a drink. If it’s valid, pick someone else to drink.

#### Round 2: Meme Drinking Games

- **Rules**: Pull a meme card or use a meme generator app. Everyone must caption it Festivus-style.
- **Drinking Challenge**: The best caption (voted by the group) gets to assign two drinks. The worst caption does a **dare challenge** from the Drinking Dojo pack.

#### Round 3: Feats of Strength (Party Edition)

- **Rules**: Instead of wrestling, compete in a quick game—flip cup, Kings, or even a **Fortnite drinking game** round.
- **Drinking Challenge**: Winner assigns three drinks. Last place takes a dare (think **NSFW dares** if your group’s up for it!).

#### Bonus: Festivus Dare Roulette

- **Rules**: Spin a bottle or use a randomizer app. The chosen one pulls a random dare card.
- **Drinking Challenge**: Complete the dare or take a double drink penalty.

## House Rules and Pro Tips

- **Keep the energy up**: Rotate games every 30 minutes for max mayhem.
- **Go viral**: Film your best moments and share them (tag #DrinkingDojo for a chance to get featured).
- **Safe vibes only**: Always look out for each other and know your limits.
