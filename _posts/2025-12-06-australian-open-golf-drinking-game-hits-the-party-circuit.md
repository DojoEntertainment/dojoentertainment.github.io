## Australian Open Golf Drinking Game Rules

Mixing classic drinking games energy with on-course action, here’s how to play:

### Drink When:
- A golfer misses a putt under 3 feet  
- The commentators mention “wind” or “greens”  
- There’s a slow-motion replay  
- A player wears a ridiculously bold outfit  
- The crowd politely claps  
- An Australian flag is shown on screen  
- Someone shouts, “Get in the hole!”

### Take Two Drinks If:
- A golfer hits their ball into the water  
- There’s a hole-in-one highlight  
- A caddie is caught on camera laughing  
- You catch a kangaroo mascot or Aussie wildlife reference

### Dare Challenges:
- Missed your turn? Pull a dare from the deck—think NSFW dares, meme dares, or whatever makes your squad crack up.  
- If your pick for the winner gets eliminated, complete a dare challenge chosen by your friends.

### Finish Your Drink If:
- The tournament ends in a tie and goes to a playoff  
- Your favorite golfer lifts the trophy (cheers to loyalty!)
