## Top Drinking Games for Eid al Fitr 2026

### 1. Crescent Cup Showdown

**What you need:**
- Solo cups (bonus points if you can find crescent moon designs)
- Ping pong balls
- Drinks of your choice

**How to play:**
1. Arrange cups in a crescent shape at each end of the table.
2. Split into two teams.
3. Take turns tossing the ball into the opposing team’s cups.
4. If you land a shot, the other team drinks.
5. First team to clear the other team’s crescent wins. Losers must complete a dare challenge (get creative with NSFW dares if your group’s into it).

### 2. Eid Meme Drinking Game

**What you need:**
- A phone or tablet with memes (or print your favorites!)
- Drinks of your choice

**How to play:**
1. Everyone submits their favorite Eid or celebration meme into a group chat or pile.
2. Each round, one person is the judge and picks the funniest meme.
3. The person who submitted the meme picks someone to drink and gives them a dare challenge.
4. Rotate judges each round and keep the laughs going.

### 3. Dare or Donate

**What you need:**
- A bowl or hat
- Slips of paper
- Pens

**How to play:**
1. Write out dares (fun, spicy, or themed for the occasion) and put them in the bowl.
2. Players take turns drawing a slip.
3. If you complete the dare, you’re safe. If you bail, take a drink!
4. For a twist, add donated coins for each failed dare and donate to a local charity at the end of the night—spread the Eid spirit while leveling up your party.
