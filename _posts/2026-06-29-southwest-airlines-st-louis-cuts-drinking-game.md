6. **Winner’s Circle**: Last one standing wins ultimate bragging rights—and maybe a free round next time you’re all grounded.

## Southwest Airlines St Louis Cuts Challenge Ideas

Mix and match these challenges or add your own for endless party drinking game fun:

- **Gate Change**: Everyone switches seats. Last person to sit drinks twice.
- **Flight Delay**: All players must freeze for 10 seconds. First to move drinks.
- **Baggage Claim**: Name three things you never want to lose while traveling. Can’t think of any? Take a sip.
- **Cancelled Flight**: The player to your left chooses an NSFW dare or drinking challenge for you.
- **Overbooked**: Two players do a rock-paper-scissors battle. Loser drinks.
- **Wedding Party Games Crossover**: Tell your most embarrassing travel story. If you refuse, drink twice.
- **Meme Drinking Games Moment**: Show the funniest travel meme on your phone. Group votes on the best—winner gives out three sips.
- **Turbulence**: Everyone drinks with their non-dominant hand for the next round.
