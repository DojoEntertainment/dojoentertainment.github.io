## Why College Football Coaching Rumors Make the Best Drinking Game

Let’s be real: nothing fires up group chats, Twitter feeds, and sports bars quite like the latest gossip about who’s moving where. So why not add some friendly drinking challenges and viral dares to the mix? With this party drinking game inspired by college football chaos, you and your crew can ride the rumor mill all night long.

## What You Need

- A TV or laptop tuned to college football news, podcasts, or Twitter/X
- Your favorite drinks (beer, seltzers, cocktails—go wild)
- Dare cards or a custom drinking game builder (like the one at [Drinking Dojo](https://drinkingdojo.com))
- Optional: Meme reaction signs for extra spice

## How to Play

Here’s how to turn every headline, leak, and hot take into the ultimate party drinking game:

### 1. Set Up Your Squad

- Gather 3+ friends (the more, the messier).
- Assign someone as the “Coach Watcher”—their job is to monitor all rumor sources.

### 2. Pick Your Poison

- Choose your drink. No pressure, but this is the time to show off your signature cocktail.
- Decide on your drinking game level: chill (sips), wild (shots), or full-on dare challenges.

### 3. The Rules

Every time one of these happens, everyone drinks:

- **Rumor Drop**: A new coach is rumored to leave or join a school—everyone takes a sip.
- **Insider Scoop**: “Sources say…” or “according to insiders”—take a gulp.
- **Old Coach Mentioned**: If an ex-coach or legend’s name pops up—cheers!
- **Denial or “No Comment”**: The school or coach denies rumors—do a group toast.
- **Wild Meme Appears**: If someone shares a meme about the rumors, the sender picks someone to do a dare.

### 4. Dare Challenges (Optional)

Spice it up with NSFW dares or meme drinking games:

- Pull a dare card from a stack (or generate one with a [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com)).
- Dares can be as wild as “Tweet your pick for next coach” or “Imitate a coach’s sideline meltdown for 30 seconds.”
- For big breaking news (like a coach actually getting fired or hired), everyone does a group challenge—try a Fortnite drinking game move or a viral TikTok dance.

### 5. Bonus Rounds

- **Rumor Bingo**: Make bingo cards with common rumor phrases. First to bingo assigns a drinking challenge.
- **Wedding Party Games Crossover**: If anyone at your party is engaged, the loser of each round has to give a wedding toast… to their favorite coach.

## Pro Tips

- Stay hydrated and snack up—coaching rumor nights can get rowdy.
- Mix in some couples drinking games if there are lovebirds in the group.
- Set a safe ride plan (Uber, cabs, or a designated driver).
