## Must-Try Drinking Games for 2025 Movie Nights

### 1. **Blockbuster Bingo**

**Materials:**  
- Blank bingo cards (or use our [custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) to generate them)  
- Pens or markers  
- Drinks of choice

**How to Play:**  
1. Before the movie, fill in your bingo cards with tropes, quotes, or moments you think will happen (e.g., “car chase,” “awkward first kiss,” “plot twist,” “epic meme moment”).  
2. Whenever one happens, mark your square and take a sip.  
3. Complete a row? Everyone else has to finish their drink or take an NSFW dare!

### 2. **Dare Roulette**

**Materials:**  
- A bowl or hat  
- Slips of paper with dares (get wild with meme drinking games or spicy NSFW dares)

**How to Play:**  
1. Pause the movie anytime a major plot twist or viral moment happens.  
2. Draw a dare from the bowl. Complete it or take a penalty drink.  
3. Add in viral drinking games dares for extra chaos (think TikTok-inspired or themed to the movie’s genre).

### 3. **Quote Chug**

**Materials:**  
- List of iconic quotes or catchphrases from the new movie (make predictions or look them up after the premiere)  
- Your favorite drink

**How to Play:**  
1. Every time a predicted quote is said, everyone takes a sip.  
2. If someone laughs or memes the line before it happens, they nominate someone to finish their drink.

### 4. **Character Challenges**

**Materials:**  
- Assign each person a character (draw names or pick favorites)  
- Drinks for everyone

**How to Play:**  
1. When your assigned character does something classic (like a catchphrase, dance, or meme-worthy fail), you drink.  
2. If another player calls out your character before you notice, you double up!  
3. The first person to lose track gets a dare challenge from the group.
