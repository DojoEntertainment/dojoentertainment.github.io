## The Main Event: Walt Disney World Drinking Game Instructions

### Setup for the Ultimate Party Drinking Games

1. Gather your group (2–10 players works best).
2. Arrange seating in a circle or around the TV.
3. Hand out drinks and set up your cards or custom game pack.

### How to Play These Viral Drinking Games

**Option 1: Movie Sips**

- Pick a Disney movie or a string of Disney park ride videos.
- Every time you spot a classic Disney trope (think: talking animal, evil laugh, musical number starts, or a character says "dream"), everyone takes a drink.
- Add meme drinking games flair by assigning a viral meme reaction to each player (e.g., when Olaf says something goofy, the "cry-laugher" downs their drink).

**Option 2: Park Adventure Dare Challenges**

- Use cards or your custom drinking game builder to create dare challenges inspired by Disney park adventures.
- Example dares:
    - Pretend you’re riding Space Mountain and act out the drops — if you laugh, take two sips.
    - Do your best villain laugh. If it’s not scary enough, finish your drink.
    - Trade drinks with a “prince” or “princess” at the table.

**Option 3: Magical Couples Drinking Games**

- Pair up! Whenever a famous Disney duo appears or is mentioned, couples toast and take a drink together.
- If someone’s “couple” gets eliminated or leaves the room, they must complete a dare challenge chosen by the group.

## Fun Dare Challenges to Try at Your Party

- **NSFW dares (for adults only):** Share your most embarrassing Disney memory or take three sips.
- **Wedding party games twist:** Whoever’s dressed most like a Disney character leads a quick group toast.
- **House party ideas:** Whoever can sing the most lyrics to “Let It Go” picks the next movie or challenge.
