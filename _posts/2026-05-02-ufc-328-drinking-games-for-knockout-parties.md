## How To Play: UFC 328 Party Drinking Game

### Basic Rules

Every fight night comes with its own set of viral moments, so we’ve built in meme drinking games and custom dares to keep things spicy. Here’s how to get started:

1. **Pick Your Fighter:** Everyone chooses a fighter in the main event. If your fighter wins, give out three drinks. If they lose, you take three drinks.  
2. **Spot the Meme:** Saw a meme-worthy knockout, awkward post-fight interview, or viral walkout? Last person to yell “MEME!” takes a drink.  
3. **Commentator Clichés:** Take a sip every time the commentators say:  
   - “He’s got a chin on him.”  
   - “That’s a huge left hand.”  
   - “Anything can happen in the UFC.”  
4. **Wild Dare Challenges:** Before the main card, each person writes down a dare (keep it NSFW if you’re feeling bold) and tosses it in a hat. Whenever there’s a split decision, the group draws a dare and the person whose fighter was in the fight has to do it.  
5. **Fight Finish:**  
   - Knockout/TKO: Everyone finishes their drink.  
   - Submission: Last person to notice the tap takes a shot.  
   - Decision: Everyone takes two sips.
