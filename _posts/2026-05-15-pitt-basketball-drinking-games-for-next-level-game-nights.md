1. **Set the Rules:** Before tip-off, use a custom drinking game builder to agree on your house rules. Keep it wild or keep it chill—just keep it fun.

### Game Play

Every time one of the following happens during the Pitt basketball game, follow the corresponding dare or challenge. Mix in some NSFW dares if your crew is up for it—just remember, consent is key!
