### 1. **Succession Drinking Game**

**Materials Needed:**  
- Drinks of choice  
- Printed or digital rules  
- Dare cards (optional for extra mayhem)

**How to Play:**  
- Every time someone says “Waystar Royco,” take a sip.  
- If Logan Roy drops an F-bomb, everyone drinks.  
- When a character double-crosses someone, pick a player to do a dare.  
- During awkward family dinners, last person to raise their drink does a challenge.

**Pro Tip:** Up the stakes with NSFW dares or meme drinking games inspired by the Roy family’s chaos.

### 2. **Euphoria Drinking Game**

**Materials Needed:**  
- Your favorite drinks  
- Euphoria-inspired dare challenges  
- A bold attitude

**How to Play:**  
- Take a drink every time there’s a party scene.  
- When Rue breaks the fourth wall, everyone sips.  
- If glitter tears make an appearance, assign a dare challenge to the most dramatic friend.  
- During every intense music montage, chug until the scene ends.

**Make It Viral:** Snap a pic of your squad’s wildest dare and share it for meme drinking game glory.

### 3. **The Last of Us Drinking Game**

**Materials Needed:**  
- Drinks  
- The Last of Us episode cued up  
- Drinking Dojo custom rule pack

**How to Play:**  
- Drink every time you spot a clicker.  
- If Joel or Ellie cracks a joke, everyone drinks.  
- When things get emotional, last person to fake cry does a dare challenge.  
- During high-tension scenes, no one can talk. First to break the silence takes a big sip.

**Couples Drinking Games Twist:** Partner up and take turns tackling dares—bonus points if you survive to the credits.
