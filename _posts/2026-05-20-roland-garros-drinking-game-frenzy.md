- A live Roland Garros match (stream or TV)
- Your fave drinks—cocktails, beer, or whatever fuels your fun
- A group of tennis-loving (or just party-loving) friends
- Cups, ping pong balls (for bonus drinking challenges)
- Optional: NSFW dares for the bold, meme cards for extra chaos

## How To Play the Roland Garros Drinking Game

### Basic Rules of This Drinking Game

1. **Serve Up the Drinks:** Everyone picks their poison and grabs a comfy seat.
2. **Choose Your Players:** Each person (or team) picks a tennis player to “represent.” If your player does something on the list below, you drink.
3. **Game On:** The match becomes your playground for drinking games, dare challenges, and viral moments.

### The Drinking Cues for This Party Drinking Game

Take a sip or complete a dare every time:

- **Ace is served:** Everyone drinks.
- **Double fault:** Player’s team drinks double.
- **Grunt fest:** If a player lets out a wild, meme-worthy grunt, the last person to mimic it drinks.
- **Racket smash:** Finish your drink or take on a dare challenge.
- **Hawk-Eye drama (review/challenge):** Predict the call. Guess wrong? Drink.
- **French crowd goes wild:** Start a mini “wave”—whoever messes up chugs.
- **Commentator says ‘clay court’:** First to yell “oui oui!” picks someone to drink.

### Dare Challenges to Amp Up Your Drinking Game

Want to kick it up a notch? Toss in some NSFW dares or couples drinking games between sets. Spin a bottle or draw from a dare deck:  
- Do your best tennis player impression  
- Attempt a “French” accent for the next round  
- Take a selfie with your drink and send it to the group chat  
- For wedding party games: Make a toast in honor of Roland Garros
