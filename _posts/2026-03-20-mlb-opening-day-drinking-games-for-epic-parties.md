## Classic MLB Drinking Game Rules

**Player count:** 2+ (the more, the better!)

### Basic Rules

Take a sip every time:
- The announcer says “Opening Day”
- A batter spits or adjusts their gear
- A foul ball hits the stands
- The camera pans to a wild fan costume

Drink twice if:
- There’s a home run
- The mascot does something ridiculous
- Someone in the room yells at the umpire

Finish your drink if:
- A player hits a grand slam
- There’s a bench-clearing moment

- **Wedding party games:** MLB Opening Day falls during wedding season. Add baseball dares to your wedding party drinking games for an unforgettable reception.
- **Drinking challenges:** Set up speed rounds — who can finish a drink before the pitcher throws three balls?
