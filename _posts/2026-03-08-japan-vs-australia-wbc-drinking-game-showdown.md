### How to Play: The Basics

This drinking game is simple to set up and even easier to play—perfect for house parties, game nights, or last-minute hangouts.

#### Basic Rules

1. **Pick Your Side:** Are you Team Japan or Team Australia? Split into two groups if you want to up the rivalry.
2. **Drink Triggers:** Every time one of the following happens, everyone on the relevant team takes a drink!
   - **Strikeout:** Team whose player struck out drinks.
   - **Home Run:** Opposing team drinks twice.
   - **Double Play:** Everyone drinks.
   - **Epic Catch:** The team whose player made the catch hands out a dare.
   - **Pitching Change:** Last person to raise their glass does a shot or a dare.
3. **Dare Challenges:** At the end of each inning, draw a card from your dare pack or use Drinking Dojo’s custom dare generator. Think NSFW dares if you’re feeling spicy, or keep it meme-worthy for viral drinking game vibes.
4. **Bonus Meme Round:** Whenever a commentator drops a meme-worthy line (“That’s a spicy pitch!”), everyone does a group cheers and takes a big sip.
