### 1. Set Up Your Brackets

Everyone fills out their own Big East tournament bracket before tip-off. Predict winners, upsets, and who’s going home early. Bonus points for creative team names and trash talk.

### 2. Assign Drinking Rules

Here’s where the party starts. Pick and choose from these rules, or add your own using our custom drinking game builder for maximum chaos:

- **Every time your predicted team loses**: Take a drink.
- **Nail a major upset**: Nominate someone else to do a dare challenge.
- **Buzzer beater**: Everyone cheers, last person to raise their drink takes two sips.
- **Airball or technical foul**: Shot for the host.
- **Announcer says “defense” or “underdog”**: Everyone drinks.
- **Meme-worthy moment (mascot dance, wild fan, coach meltdown)**: Meme drinking games time! Best meme wins, loser does NSFW dares or finishes their drink.
