## Tennis Drinking Game Rules

### Classic Tennis Sips

1. **Sip Whenever…**
   - The commentator says “unforced error”
   - There’s a rally over 10 shots
   - A player argues with the umpire

2. **Take a Shot If…**
   - Someone wins a set 6–0 (a “bagel”)
   - A player smashes their racket
   - There’s a challenge and the call is overturned

3. **Group Dare Challenges**
   - Every time a player does a celebratory fist pump, the group must complete a **meme drinking game** dare. Use your **custom drinking game builder** to add in spicy or **NSFW dares** if you’re feeling bold.

### Drinking Dojo Tennis Showdown

Want to take things up a notch? Split your group into “Team Serve” and “Team Return.” Every time your team’s player wins a point, the other team drinks. For extra laughs, throw in viral drinking games rules like:

- **Fortnite drinking game** crossover: Every ace is a “Victory Royale”—everyone but the server drinks.
- **Wedding party games** twist: If someone mentions “love” (the tennis score, not the feeling), everyone gives a toast before sipping.

### Tennis Dare Court

Between sets, draw dare challenges from a hat — think “do your best tennis grunt,” “chug with your non-dominant hand,” or “recreate a famous match point celebration.” For **couples drinking games** or smaller groups, dare each other to serve up antics that’ll get the whole **house party** talking.

## House Party Ideas for Tennis Fans

- **Dress code:** Come decked out in tennis whites or your favorite player’s gear.
- **DIY meme drinking games:** Mix in viral dares or custom inside jokes.
- **Tennis trivia:** Wrong answers = drinks for your team.
