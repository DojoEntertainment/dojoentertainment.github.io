### Basic Rules

1. **Pick Your Event**  
   Choose which Olympic events you’ll watch—track, swimming, gymnastics, you name it.

2. **Choose Your Game Style**  
   - **Classic Sips:** Take a sip when specific things happen (see below).  
   - **Dare Challenges:** Pull a dare card when a medal is awarded.  
   - **Meme Mode:** Assign a meme reaction or inside joke to certain athletes or commentators.

3. **Set Your Boundaries**  
   Always drink responsibly! Know your limits and keep water close.

### Drinking Cues (Sample List)

- Take a sip every time an athlete sets a new record.  
- Take two sips if your country wins a medal.  
- Drink when a commentator uses a sports cliché (“gave 110 percent,” anyone?).  
- Pull a dare challenge if someone falls or wipes out.  
- Shot for a surprise tie or upset.

### Dare Challenge Ideas

- Recite the Olympic oath in your best dramatic voice.  
- Do ten jumping jacks (bonus points if you spill nothing).  
- Post a meme-worthy selfie to your group chat.  
- Swap drinks with the person to your left for the next round.  
- NSFW dares: Only if everyone’s game—think truth-or-dare level, but with a competitive twist.
