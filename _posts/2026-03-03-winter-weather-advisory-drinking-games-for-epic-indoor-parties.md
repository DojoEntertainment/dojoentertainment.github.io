## 1. Weather the Storm: Classic Drinking Challenge

**What you’ll need:**
- Deck of cards  
- Your favorite drinks  
- Good vibes  

**How to play:**
1. Everyone sits in a circle.
2. Place the deck of cards in the center.
3. Players take turns drawing a card and following these rules:
   - **Red Cards:** Take a sip.
   - **Black Cards:** Give a sip to someone else.
   - **Face Cards:** Dare challenge—draw a card from a separate pile of dares (think NSFW dares or meme-related tasks for extra spice).
   - **Aces:** Start a waterfall (everyone drinks until the person before them stops).
4. If someone refuses a dare, they take a double drink.

**Why it slaps:**  
It’s easy, flexible, and you can throw in custom dares or memes to keep things wild. Plus, everyone gets involved—no wallflowers here.

## 2. Meme Blizzard: Viral Drinking Game

**What you’ll need:**
- Printed memes (or just your phones)
- Drinks for everyone

**How to play:**
1. Each person finds a meme that sums up winter weather struggles (think slipping on ice, shoveling driveways, messy hair days).
2. Take turns sharing your meme with the group.
3. The group rates the meme from 1 (meh) to 10 (legendary).
4. For every point under 7, the person who shared the meme takes a drink. If you hit a 10, everyone else drinks.

## 3. Snowed-In Dare Roulette

**What you’ll need:**
- Bowl full of pre-written dares (include some NSFW dares for the brave)
- Drinks

**How to play:**
1. Write down wild dare challenges on slips of paper and toss them in a bowl.
2. Players take turns drawing a dare.
3. Complete the dare or take a penalty drink.
4. After every round, add new dares to keep things unpredictable.

**Pro tip:**  
Make it extra spicy with dares inspired by your own inside jokes or viral trends from TikTok and Instagram.

## 4. Couples Chill: Cozy Drinking Games

**What you’ll need:**
- Your partner
- Drinks
- Blanket fort (optional but highly recommended)

**How to play:**
1. Play “Never Have I Ever” with a winter twist—think “Never have I ever slipped on ice,” or “Never have I ever missed a snow day party.”
2. For every “I have,” take a sip.
3. Winner gets to pick a cozy dare for the other.

**Why try it:**  
Perfect for snowed-in date nights or couples who love a good laugh.
