## How To Play The Election Day Drinking Game

Here’s a party drinking game that’ll have everyone invested in both the results and the next round. Use our classic rules or hit up [Drinking Dojo’s custom drinking game builder](https://drinkingdojo.com) to make your own legendary set.

### Basic Rules for Your Election Day Drinking Game

Take a sip, shot, or dare every time…

- A candidate says “the American people” (or “the British people,” “Canadians,” “Australians,” or “Danes” depending on your country)
- There’s a technical glitch or awkward silence
- A news anchor says “too close to call”
- Someone mentions a viral meme or trending hashtag
- The camera pans to a dramatic crowd reaction
- A politician dodges a question
- Your least favorite party wins a state/region/constituency
- Someone in the room yells at the TV

### Dare Challenges to Amp Up Your Election Day Drinking Game

Want to spice things up? Add dare challenges for next-level chaos:

- **NSFW dares:** The loser of a round has to do a wild dare (keep it safe and consensual!)
- **Meme challenge:** Quickest person to share a political meme in the group chat picks someone to drink
- **Impersonation round:** Mimic a politician’s speech—if no one laughs, you drink

### Bonus Rounds for Epic Election Day Drinking Games

- **Victory Speech:** When a candidate gives their victory speech, everyone must toast. Last person to stand (or sit up) drinks twice!
- **Swing State Showdown:** Every time a swing state is called, everyone picks a side. The losing side drinks.
