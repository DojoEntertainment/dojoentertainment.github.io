## How to Play: The Iga Swiatek Drinking Game Rules

This party drinking game is all about matching Iga’s energy, shot for shot — and drink for drink. Here’s how it works:

### Basic Rules

- **Pick Your Player:** Everyone chooses either Iga Swiatek or her opponent. If you’re a big group, split up and form Team Iga vs. Team Challenger.  
- **Drink on These Events:**  
  - **Iga Wins a Point:** Take one sip.  
  - **Iga Hits an Ace:** Everyone cheers and takes two sips.  
  - **Iga Breaks Serve:** Team Iga gives a dare challenge to Team Challenger.  
  - **Opponent Double Faults:** Last person to react drinks.  
  - **Iga Does Her Signature Fist Pump:** Everyone must do their best impersonation and the worst one takes a dare.  
  - **Set Won by Iga:** Everyone on Team Challenger finishes their drink or does an NSFW dare.  
- **Meme Moment:** If the commentators mention anything about Iga’s age, ranking, or dominance, everyone drinks!
